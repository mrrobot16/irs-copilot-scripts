This document is the Form 1099-SB, which is used to report the seller's investment in a life insurance contract. It is to be filed with the Internal Revenue Service (IRS) and furnished to the seller. The form includes information such as the issuer's name, address, and taxpayer identification number (TIN), the seller's TIN, name, and address, as well as the policy number. It also includes boxes to report the investment in the contract and surrender amount. There are separate copies of the form for the IRS, the seller, and the issuer. The instructions provide guidance on completing the form and filing and furnishing requirements. The document also includes contact information for assistance with reporting on Form 1099-SB.
