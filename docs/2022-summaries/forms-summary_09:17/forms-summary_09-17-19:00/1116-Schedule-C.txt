The document is a Schedule C (Form 1116) for reporting foreign tax redeterminations to the Internal Revenue Service (IRS). The form is used to report changes in the amount of foreign taxes accrued or paid by a taxpayer for each applicable category of income. The form includes sections for reporting increases and decreases in foreign taxes, as well as changes in foreign tax credits claimed and changes in U.S. tax liability. There is also a section for annual reporting of contested taxes for future tax years. The form provides spaces to enter information such as the tax year, payor name and identification number, country or U.S. possession where the tax is paid, and the amount of tax paid or accrued in local currency and U.S. dollars. The form also includes instructions and references to additional resources on the IRS website for guidance on completing the form.
