This document is Form 2553, which is used to elect S corporation status for a small business corporation under section 1362 of the Internal Revenue Code. The form provides instructions on where to file it based on the location of the corporation's principal business, office, or agency. It also includes sections for providing election information such as the corporation's name, address, and tax year, as well as a consent statement from shareholders. There is also a section for selecting a fiscal tax year and making a QSST (Qualified Subchapter S Trust) election if applicable. Additionally, there is a section for making representations for a late corporate classification election. The form must be signed by an officer or legal representative and contains a declaration under penalties of perjury.
