The document is a collection of forms and instructions related to the Heavy Highway Vehicle Use Tax Return (Form 2290) for the tax period beginning on July 1, 2021, and ending on June 30, 2022. The document provides information on how to complete the form, including instructions on figuring the tax, reporting suspended vehicles, and completing Schedule 1. It also includes a consent form for the disclosure of tax information to the Department of Transportation, U.S. Customs and Border Protection, and state Departments of Motor Vehicles. Additionally, there is a payment voucher (Form 2290-V) for those making a payment by check or money order. The document provides instructions on how to complete the voucher and where to send it along with the payment.
