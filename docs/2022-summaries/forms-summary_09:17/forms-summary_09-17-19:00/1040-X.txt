Form 1040-X is the Amended U.S. Individual Income Tax Return, used to make changes to a previously filed tax return for the years 2019 or later. The form includes sections for personal information, filing status, income and deductions, tax liability, payments, and refund or amount owed. It also includes sections for dependents and an explanation of the changes being made. The form must be signed by the taxpayer and, if applicable, their spouse. The form is to be filed with supporting documents and any new or changed forms and schedules.
