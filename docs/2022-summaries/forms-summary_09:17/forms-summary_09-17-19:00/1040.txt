Form 1040 is the U.S. Individual Income Tax Return form used by taxpayers to report their income and calculate their tax liability for the year. The document includes various sections and fields for taxpayers to fill out, including personal information, filing status, income sources, deductions, tax credits, and payments made. The form also provides instructions and additional schedules for taxpayers to use as needed. It is important for taxpayers to accurately complete and sign the form, as it is a legal declaration of their income and tax liability.
