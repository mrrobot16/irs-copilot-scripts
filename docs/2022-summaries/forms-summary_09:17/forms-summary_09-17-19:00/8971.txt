This document provides updated information regarding the address to file Form 8971, which is used to report information about beneficiaries acquiring property from a deceased individual. The updated address is the Department of the Treasury, Internal Revenue Service, Stop 8307F, 7940 Kentucky Drive, Florence, KY 41042-2915. The document includes a form with sections for providing information about the decedent and executor, as well as the beneficiaries. It also includes a schedule for reporting details about the property acquired from the decedent, including its description, valuation date, and estate tax value. The document emphasizes that only Schedule A of Form 8971 should be provided to the beneficiaries and that Form 8971 should be submitted to the IRS along with a copy of each completed Schedule A.
