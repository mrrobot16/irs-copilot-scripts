The document is a form called Schedule A (Form 1040) for itemized deductions. It is used to report various deductions on a taxpayer's federal income tax return. The form includes sections for medical and dental expenses, taxes paid, interest paid, gifts to charity, casualty and theft losses, and other itemized deductions. Taxpayers are instructed to attach this form to their Form 1040 or 1040-SR. The form also provides cautionary notes and references to additional instructions and resources for further guidance.
