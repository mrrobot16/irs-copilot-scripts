This document provides instructions for employers on how to file Forms W-2 and W-3 electronically with the Social Security Administration (SSA). It also provides information on how to order official IRS information returns and explains the purpose of each section on Form W-2GU, which is specific to Guam. The document also includes instructions for employees on how to file their tax returns and how to interpret the information on Form W-2GU. It emphasizes the importance of keeping copies of Form W-2GU for record-keeping purposes.
