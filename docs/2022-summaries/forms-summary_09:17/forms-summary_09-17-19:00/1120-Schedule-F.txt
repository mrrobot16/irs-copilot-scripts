Form 1120-SF is the U.S. Income Tax Return for Settlement Funds under Section 468B. It is used to report income and deductions for settlement funds. The form includes sections for reporting income such as taxable interest, dividends, capital gains, income or gain from partnership interests, and other income. Deductions can be claimed for trustee/administrator fees, taxes, accounting and legal services, notification of claimants and claim processing expenses, and other deductions. The form also includes a tax computation section to calculate the modified gross income, total tax, and any credits and payments. The form requires information about assets, liabilities, and fund balance, as well as additional information about transfers to the fund, tax-exempt interest, distributions to claimants, distributions to transferors or related parties, liabilities for which the fund was established, and court order number if applicable. The form must be signed by the fund administrator.
