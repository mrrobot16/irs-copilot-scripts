Form 8937 is a document issued by the Department of the Treasury and Internal Revenue Service (IRS) in the United States. It is used to report organizational actions that affect the basis of securities for tax purposes. The form requires information about the issuer, such as their name, employer identification number (EIN), contact information, and the date of the action. It also requires details about the organizational action, including a description, its quantitative effect on the basis of the security, and the calculation of the change in basis. The form also asks for the applicable Internal Revenue Code section(s) and subsection(s) upon which the tax treatment is based, and whether any resulting loss can be recognized. The form must be signed and submitted to the IRS along with any accompanying statements.
