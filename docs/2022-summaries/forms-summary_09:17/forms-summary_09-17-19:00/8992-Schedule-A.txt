Schedule A (Form 8992) is a document provided by the Internal Revenue Service (IRS) for taxpayers to report information related to Controlled Foreign Corporations (CFCs) and compute Global Intangible Low-Taxed Income (GILTI). The form contains various sections for individuals to enter calculations and allocations for net tested income and GILTI allocated to tested income CFCs. The form includes spaces to provide details such as the name of the CFC, EIN or Reference ID, tested income, tested loss, pro rata share of tested income and loss, qualified business asset investment (QBAI), tested interest income and expense, GILTI allocation ratio, and GILTI allocated to tested income CFCs. The form also includes continuation sheets for additional space if needed. The totals from the various sections should be included on line 1 of the form. The document provides a reference to the IRS website for instructions and the latest information on completing the form.
