Form 6069 is a tax return form used by certain individuals and entities to report and pay excise taxes under sections 4951, 4952, and 4953 of the Internal Revenue Code. The form includes sections for providing information about the filer, such as their name, address, and taxpayer identification number. It also includes sections for reporting and calculating the initial taxes on self-dealing, taxable expenditures, and coal mine operators. The form requires the filer to provide details about the acts of self-dealing, taxable expenditures, and excess contributions, as well as any corrective action taken. The form also includes sections for the filer to sign and declare the accuracy of the information provided.
