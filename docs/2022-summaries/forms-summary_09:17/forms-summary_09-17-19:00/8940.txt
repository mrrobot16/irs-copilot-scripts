This document provides important update information regarding certain tax forms and their new mailing addresses. The new mailing address for forms such as 1023, 1024, 1024-A, 1028, 5300, 5307, 5310, 5310-A, 5316, 8717, 8718, and 8940 is provided. The document also mentions that deliveries by private delivery service should be made to a different address. The update is meant to supplement the instructions for these forms and should be relied upon until the next revision of the forms' instructions. The document then provides the details of Form 8940, which is a request for miscellaneous determination under various sections of the Internal Revenue Code. It includes sections for identifying the organization, providing contact information, and selecting the type of request being made. The form must be signed by an authorized official.
