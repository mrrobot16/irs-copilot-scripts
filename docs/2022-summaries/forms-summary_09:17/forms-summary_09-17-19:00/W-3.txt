However, it notes that Copy A of the form provided on the website is not scannable and should not be filed with the SSA. The document also includes information on ordering official IRS information returns, penalties for filing non-scannable forms, and contact information for the IRS. It provides a sample Form W-3 and instructions on how to complete and file it. It also mentions the option to e-file the forms through the SSA's Business Services Online (BSO) website and provides details on the e-filing options available. The document specifies the deadline for filing paper forms and provides the address for mailing them to the SSA. It also includes information on using certified mail or an IRS-approved private delivery service. The document concludes with a notice regarding the Privacy Act and Paperwork Reduction Act.
