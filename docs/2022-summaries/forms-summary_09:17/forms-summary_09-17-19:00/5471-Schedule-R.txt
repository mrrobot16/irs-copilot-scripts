Schedule R (Form 5471) is a document issued by the Department of the Treasury, Internal Revenue Service (IRS) in the United States. It is used to report distributions from a foreign corporation and must be attached to Form 5471.

The form requires the following information to be provided:
1. Name of the person filing Form 5471.
2. Identifying number of the person filing Form 5471.
3. Name of the foreign corporation.
4. Employer Identification Number (EIN) of the foreign corporation, if applicable.
5. Reference ID number (as per the instructions).

The form also provides a table with multiple columns to report the details of each distribution from the foreign corporation. The columns include:
(a) Description of the distribution.
(b) Date of the distribution.
(c) Amount of the distribution in the foreign corporation's functional currency.
(d) Amount of earnings and profits (E&P) distribution in the foreign corporation's functional currency.

The document also includes a section for the Paperwork Reduction Act Notice, which provides instructions and information regarding the reduction of paperwork burden.

Taxpayers can visit the IRS website at www.irs.gov/Form5471 for detailed instructions and the latest information related to Schedule R (Form 5471).
