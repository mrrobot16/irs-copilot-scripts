Form 1042-S is a document issued by the Internal Revenue Service (IRS) for reporting foreign persons' U.S. source income subject to withholding. It contains various boxes for reporting income codes, gross income, chapter indicators, exemption codes, tax rates, withholding allowances, net income, federal tax withheld, and other relevant information. The form is used by withholding agents to report income and withholding information to both the IRS and the recipients of the income. It is also used by recipients to attach to their federal and state tax returns. The form includes instructions and codes to help categorize and report different types of income and withholding.
