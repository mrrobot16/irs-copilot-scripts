Schedule P (Form 8865) is a document provided by the Internal Revenue Service (IRS) for reporting acquisitions, dispositions, and changes of interests in a foreign partnership under Section 6046A of the tax code. This schedule is to be attached to Form 8865.

The form requires the following information to be provided:

Part I: Acquisitions
- Name, address, and identification number of the person from whom the interest was acquired
- Date of acquisition
- Fair market value (FMV) of the interest acquired
- Basis in the interest acquired
- Percentage of interest before and after the acquisition

Part II: Dispositions
- Name, address, and identification number of the person who acquired the interest
- Date of disposition
- FMV of the interest disposed
- Basis in the interest disposed
- Percentage of interest before and after the disposition

Part III: Change in Proportional Interest
- Description of the change
- Date of the change
- FMV of the interest
- Basis in the interest
- Percentage of interest before and after the change

Part IV: Supplemental Information Required To Be Reported

The form also includes a reference ID number and space for the name of the person filing Form 8865, their identification number, name of the foreign partnership, and its Employer Identification Number (EIN) if applicable.

The form is accompanied by instructions that can be found on the IRS website (www.irs.gov/Form8865) and should be used for the latest information.

This form is used to accurately report acquisitions, dispositions, and changes of interests in a foreign partnership, ensuring compliance with tax regulations.
