Form 7004 is an application for an automatic extension of time to file certain business income tax, information, and other returns. This form is used to request an extension for various types of tax returns, such as Form 1065, Form 1120, Form 1041, and others. The form requires the taxpayer to provide their name, identifying number, address, and the form code for the return they are requesting an extension for. The form also includes a section for all filers to complete, which includes checkboxes for specific situations, such as being a foreign corporation without a US office or being the common parent of a group intending to file a consolidated return. The taxpayer must also indicate the tax year the extension is for and provide information on their tentative total tax, total payments and credits, and balance due. The form includes instructions and a website link for additional information.
