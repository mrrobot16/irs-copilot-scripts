This document provides information about Form 1098, which is a Mortgage Interest Statement. It explains that Copy A of the form is for informational purposes only and cannot be scanned if printed from the website. It warns against filing Copy A downloaded from the website, as a penalty may be imposed. However, Copy B and other copies of the form can be downloaded, printed, and used to provide information to the recipient. It also provides instructions for ordering official IRS information returns and filing electronically. The document includes the form itself, along with instructions for the payer/borrower and recipient/lender on how to complete the form. It also provides contact information for assistance with reporting on Form 1098.
