It is used by individuals, partnerships, corporations, and other entities to request approval from the Internal Revenue Service (IRS) to adopt, change, or retain a specific tax year. The form consists of several sections where the applicant provides general information, indicates the type of applicant, and requests automatic approval or a ruling from the IRS.
