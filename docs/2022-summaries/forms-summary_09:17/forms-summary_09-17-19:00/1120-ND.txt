Form 1120-ND is a tax return form for Nuclear Decommissioning Funds and certain related persons. It is used to report income, deductions, and tax payments for the fund. The form includes sections for computing fund income tax, reporting balance sheets, and providing other information such as contributions received, self-dealing acts, and initial taxes on self-dealing. The form must be signed by an officer of the fund and can be prepared by a paid preparer.
