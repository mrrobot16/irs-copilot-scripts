Form 5500-EZ is an annual return form that must be filed by owners/partners and their spouses who have a one-participant retirement plan or a foreign plan. This form is required under section 6058(a) of the Internal Revenue Code. The form includes sections for identifying information, basic plan information, financial information, plan characteristics, and compliance and funding questions. The form must be completed in accordance with the provided instructions and can be found on the IRS website. The form is open to public inspection.
