Form 1094-B is a transmittal form used by filers to submit health coverage information returns to the Internal Revenue Service (IRS). The form is provided by the Department of the Treasury and can be accessed on the IRS website. The form includes sections for the filer's name, employer identification number (EIN), contact information, and address. There is also a space to indicate the total number of Forms 1095-B that are being submitted with the transmittal. The form must be signed and dated by the filer, who declares under penalties of perjury that the information provided is true, correct, and complete. The form also includes a notice regarding the Privacy Act and Paperwork Reduction Act. This particular version of the form is for the year 2022.
