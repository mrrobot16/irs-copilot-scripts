Form 8023 is a document provided by the Internal Revenue Service (IRS) for corporations making qualified stock purchases and wishing to make elections under Section 338 of the tax code. The form requires information about the purchasing corporation, the common parent of the purchasing corporation, and the target corporation. It also includes sections for completing information about the common parent of the selling consolidated group, selling affiliate, S corporation shareholder, or U.S. shareholder. The form also includes sections for general information and making elections under Section 338. The form must be signed by authorized individuals representing the purchasing corporation, the common parent or selling affiliate, and the S corporation shareholder(s). The form is used for tax purposes and should be filed with the IRS.
