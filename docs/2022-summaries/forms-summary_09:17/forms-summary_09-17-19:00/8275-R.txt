Form 8275-R is a regulation disclosure statement used by taxpayers to disclose items or positions on their tax return that are contrary to Treasury regulations. This form is specifically for disclosures related to foreign entities. It is important to attach this form to the tax return. The form has various sections for providing detailed information about the items being disclosed, including the regulation section, item or group of items, detailed description, form or schedule, and line number. There is also a section for providing a detailed explanation of the disclosed items. If the disclosure is related to a pass-through entity, such as a partnership or S corporation, there is a section for providing information about that entity. The form also includes space for additional explanations if needed. The form is accompanied by separate instructions and includes a Paperwork Reduction Act Notice.
