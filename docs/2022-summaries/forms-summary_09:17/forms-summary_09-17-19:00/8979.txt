Form 8979 is a document provided by the Internal Revenue Service (IRS) for partnerships to use when revoking or resigning a partnership representative or designated individual. The form is used to designate a new representative or individual and appoint a successor if necessary. There are various sections on the form for different scenarios, including revoking an entity partnership representative, revoking an individual partnership representative, resigning a partnership representative or designated individual, and designating a new partnership representative. The form requires the completion of personal and contact information for the individuals involved, as well as their taxpayer identification numbers. The form must be signed by an authorized person and includes a declaration under penalties of perjury. The form is dated and includes space for the printed or typed name of the authorized person.
