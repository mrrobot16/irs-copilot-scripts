Form 8853 is a document provided by the Internal Revenue Service (IRS) for individuals to report their Archer Medical Savings Account (MSA) contributions and deductions, as well as their Long-Term Care (LTC) insurance contracts. The form is to be attached to Form 1040, 1040-SR, or 1040-NR when filing taxes.

The form is divided into three sections: A, B, and C.

Section A is for individuals with Archer MSAs. It requires information on employer contributions, personal contributions, limitations, and deductions related to Archer MSAs.

Section B is for individuals with Medicare Advantage MSAs. It requires information on distributions received, qualified medical expenses, taxable distributions, and any additional taxes.

Section C is for individuals with LTC insurance contracts. It requires information on payments received, accelerated death benefits, costs incurred for qualified LTC services, reimbursements, and taxable payments.

The form includes instructions for each section, and individuals must follow these instructions carefully to accurately report their MSA contributions and deductions, as well as their LTC insurance contracts.

It is important to note that this summary provides a general overview of Form 8853, and individuals should consult the official instructions and the IRS website for the most up-to-date and detailed information.
