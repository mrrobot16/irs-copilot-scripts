Form 1042 is an annual withholding tax return for U.S. source income of foreign persons. The form is used by withholding agents to report and remit taxes withheld on income payments made to foreign persons. The form requires information such as the name and identification number of the withholding agent, the amount of tax liability for each period, the number of Forms 1042-S filed, and the total gross amounts reported on all Forms 1042-S and 1000. The form also includes sections for the reconciliation of payments of U.S. source FDAP income and for reporting potential section 871(m) transactions and dividend equivalent payments by a qualified derivatives dealer. The form must be signed and filed under penalties of perjury.
