This document provides instructions for filing Forms W-2 and W-3 electronically with the Social Security Administration (SSA). It states that you can create fill-in versions of the forms for filing with the SSA and print out copies for filing with state or local governments, distribution to employees, and for your records. It also emphasizes that Copy A of the form is for informational purposes only and should not be filed with the SSA as it is not scannable. The document provides information on ordering official IRS information returns and suggests referring to IRS Publications 1141, 1167, and 1179 for more information on printing tax forms. The document also includes a Form W-3SS, which is the transmittal form for filing paper copies of Forms W-2AS, W-2CM, W-2GU, or W-2VI. It provides instructions on when and where to file the paper forms and recommends e-filing options provided by the SSA. Finally, the document provides addresses for filing Copy 1 of Form W-3SS and the associated forms with state, city, or local tax departments in American Samoa, Guam, U.S. Virgin Islands, and the Commonwealth of the Northern Mariana Islands.
