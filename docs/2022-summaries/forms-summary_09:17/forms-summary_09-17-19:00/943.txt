Form 943 is the Employers Annual Federal Tax Return for Agricultural Employees for the year 2022. It is used by employers to report wages paid to agricultural employees and calculate the taxes owed. The form includes sections for entering the number of agricultural employees, wages subject to social security and Medicare taxes, federal income tax withheld, and various tax credits. The form also includes a payment voucher (Form 943-V) for making a payment with the return. The form must be completed in its entirety and signed by the taxpayer.
