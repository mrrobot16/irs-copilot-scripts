Form SS-8 is a document used by the Internal Revenue Service (IRS) to determine the worker status for purposes of federal employment taxes and income tax withholding. The form is to be completed by either the firm or the worker and includes general information about the firm and worker, as well as details about the work performed and the relationship between the firm and the worker. The form also includes sections related to behavioral control, financial control, and the relationship between the worker and the firm. Additionally, there is a section specifically for service providers or salespersons. The form must be completed accurately and truthfully, and it is signed under penalties of perjury.
