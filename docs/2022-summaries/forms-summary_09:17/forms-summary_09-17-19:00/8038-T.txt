Form 8038-T is a document provided by the Internal Revenue Service (IRS) for reporting arbitrage rebate, yield reduction, and penalty in lieu of arbitrage rebate. It is used by issuers to provide information related to these payments and penalties. The form includes sections for reporting the issuer's name, employer identification number (EIN), address, and contact information. It also includes sections for reporting the amount of rebate and yield reduction payments, as well as penalty payments. There is a section for reporting late payments and whether they qualify for a waiver of penalty. The form also includes sections for reporting total payment, unspent proceeds, proceeds used to redeem bonds, and other miscellaneous information. The form must be signed and dated by the issuer's authorized representative.
