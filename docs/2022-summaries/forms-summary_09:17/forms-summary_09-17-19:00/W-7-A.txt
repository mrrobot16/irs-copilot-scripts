Form W-7A is an application form issued by the Internal Revenue Service (IRS) for individuals who are in the process of adopting a child and need a Taxpayer Identification Number (TIN) for the child. The form is used specifically for pending U.S. adoptions. The form requires information about the adoptive parents, including their names, social security numbers, and address. It also requires information about the child, such as their name, birth information, and placement agency information. The form must be signed by the adoptive parent(s) and requires the attachment of necessary documentation. The form includes a declaration of truthfulness and completeness, and the applicant is advised to keep a copy of the form for their records.
