Form 8936-A is a document provided by the Department of the Treasury and the Internal Revenue Service (IRS) for taxpayers to claim the Qualified Commercial Clean Vehicle Credit. This form should be attached to the taxpayer's tax return. The instructions and the latest information can be found on the IRS website at www.irs.gov/Form8936A.

Taxpayers should use this form to claim the credit for qualified commercial clean vehicles. The credit for certain plug-in and new clean vehicles should be claimed on Form 8936.

On the form, taxpayers are required to enter the total credit amount figured on Schedule 1 (Form 8936-A). Additionally, there is a section for partnerships and S corporations to report the qualified commercial clean vehicle credit.

Partnerships and S corporations should add lines 1 and 2 and report this amount on Schedule K. All other taxpayers should report this amount on Form 3800, Part III, line 1zz.

It is important to note that a separate Schedule 1 (Form 8936-A) should be completed for each qualified commercial clean vehicle placed in service during the tax year.

The form includes a Paperwork Reduction Act Notice, and taxpayers should refer to the separate instructions for more details.

This summary provides a brief overview of Form 8936-A. For complete and accurate information, taxpayers should refer to the instructions and the latest information provided on the IRS website.
