This document is a Schedule C form for partnerships to attach to Form 1065. It is intended for Schedule M-3 filers and provides additional information. The form asks a series of yes or no questions regarding transfers between the partnership and its partners subject to disclosure requirements, disproportionate allocations from another partnership, transfers of intangible assets to related persons, acquisitions of intangible assets from related persons, changes in accounting principles for financial accounting purposes, and changes in accounting methods for US income tax purposes. The form also includes a notice for the Paperwork Reduction Act.
