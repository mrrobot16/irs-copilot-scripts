Form 5713 is a document used by individuals, partnerships, corporations, trusts, and estates to report any operations or participation in international boycotts for tax purposes. The form requires the filer to provide their identifying information, as well as information about their total assets, ordinary income, and tax benefits related to foreign tax credits and deferrals. The form also asks specific questions about operations in or related to boycotting countries, including Israel, as well as nonlisted countries and other countries that require participation in international boycotts. The form requires the filer to disclose any requests or agreements related to participation in or cooperation with an international boycott. If the filer answers yes to participating in a boycott, they must attach copies of any boycott clauses agreed to and provide a general statement of the agreement.
