Form 1120-FSC is the U.S. Income Tax Return of a Foreign Sales Corporation. This form is used by foreign sales corporations to report their income and calculate their tax liability to the IRS. The form requires information such as the corporation's name, address, and identification number, as well as details about its assets and shareholders. The form also includes schedules for reporting cost of goods sold, taxable income, deductions, and balance sheets. The form is used to reconcile the corporation's income per books with its income per the tax return, and to analyze the changes in its retained earnings.
