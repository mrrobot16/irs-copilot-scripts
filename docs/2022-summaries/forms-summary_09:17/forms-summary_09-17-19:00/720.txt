Form 720 is a Quarterly Federal Excise Tax Return used to report and pay federal excise taxes. The form includes various sections for reporting different types of excise taxes, such as environmental taxes, communications and air transportation taxes, fuel taxes, foreign insurance taxes, manufacturers taxes, and more. Each section requires the taxpayer to provide information on the type of tax, rate, and amount owed. The form also includes schedules for reporting excise tax liability, claims, and other information. Form 720-V is a payment voucher that can be used when making a payment by check or money order with Form 720.
