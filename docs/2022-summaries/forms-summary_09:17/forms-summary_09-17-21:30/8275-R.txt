Form 8275-R is a disclosure statement form used by taxpayers to disclose items or positions on their tax return that are contrary to Treasury regulations. This form is specifically used for disclosures related to information returns for foreign entities. The form requires the taxpayer to provide general information about the regulation section, item or group of items, detailed description, form or schedule, and line number. Additionally, there is a section for a detailed explanation and information about pass-through entities if applicable. The form must be attached to the taxpayer's tax return.
