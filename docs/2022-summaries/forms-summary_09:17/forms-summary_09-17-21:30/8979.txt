Form 8979 is a document provided by the Internal Revenue Service (IRS) for partnerships to revoke, designate, or resign a partnership representative. The form includes several sections for the partnership to provide information about their current representative, as well as the individual or entity being designated or appointed as a successor representative. The form also includes signature sections for the authorized person to declare their authority and sign the form. The purpose of this form is to ensure that the partnership has a designated representative for tax purposes.
