Form SS-8 is a document used by the Internal Revenue Service (IRS) to determine the worker status for purposes of federal employment taxes and income tax withholding. The form collects information about the firm or person for whom the worker performed services, as well as the worker's personal information. The form also includes sections for disclosing information and completing questions related to the worker's employment status, behavioral control, financial control, and the relationship between the worker and the firm. The form must be completed accurately and signed under penalties of perjury.
