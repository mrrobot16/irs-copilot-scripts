Form 1042 is an annual withholding tax return for U.S. source income of foreign persons. It is used by withholding agents to report and remit taxes withheld on payments made to foreign persons. The form includes various sections for recording federal tax liability, total gross amounts reported on Forms 1042-S and 1000, tax withheld by the withholding agent, adjustments to withholding, computation of tax due or overpayment, and reconciliation of payments of U.S. source FDAP income. The form also includes sections for potential Section 871(m) transactions and dividend equivalent payments by a Qualified Derivatives Dealer (QDD). The form must be signed by the withholding agent and is subject to penalties of perjury.
