Form 8865 is a tax form used by U.S. persons who have a financial interest in certain foreign partnerships. The form is used to report information about the foreign partnership, including its tax year, share of liabilities, and other partners involved. The form also requires information about the foreign partnership's organization, principal place of business, and business activities. Additionally, the form asks for information about the partnership's agent in the United States and in the country of organization, as well as the custody of the partnership's books and records. The form also includes questions about disallowed deductions, special allocations, foreign disregarded entities, and the classification of the partnership under the laws of the country in which it is organized. The form also includes schedules for reporting constructive ownership of partnership interests, certain partners of the foreign partnership, foreign partners of section 721(c) partnerships, and an affiliation schedule. The form also includes schedules for reporting income, deductions, and balance sheets per books, as well as reconciling income per books with income per tax return and analyzing partners' capital accounts. The form also includes a schedule for reporting transactions between the controlled foreign partnership and related entities.
