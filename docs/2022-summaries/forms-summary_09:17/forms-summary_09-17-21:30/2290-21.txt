This document is a coversheet for Form 2290, which is the Heavy Highway Vehicle Use Tax Return. The document provides information about the revision date, which is July 2021, and states that it is applicable for the tax period beginning on July 1, 2021, and ending on June 30, 2022. It advises not to use this revision if filing a return for a tax period that began on or before June 30, 2021. The document also provides a website where previous revisions of Form 2290 and its instructions can be obtained. The coversheet includes a blank Form 2290 with instructions for filling it out, as well as a Schedule 1 form for reporting heavy highway vehicles. It also includes a consent form for the disclosure of tax information to the Department of Transportation, U.S. Customs and Border Protection, and state Departments of Motor Vehicles. Lastly, it includes a payment voucher for those making a payment by check or money order.
