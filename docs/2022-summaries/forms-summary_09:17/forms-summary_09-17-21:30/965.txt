Form 965 is a document provided by the Internal Revenue Service (IRS) for taxpayers to report the inclusion of deferred foreign income upon transition to the Participation Exemption System. The form is to be attached to the taxpayer's tax return.

The form consists of three parts: Section 965(a) Inclusion, Section 965(c) Deduction, and Elections. However, the specific details and instructions for each section are not provided in the summarized document.

The form also includes spaces for the taxpayer's name, identifying number, and the tax year for which the form is being filed.

Taxpayers are instructed to refer to the IRS website, www.irs.gov/Form965, for complete instructions and the latest information regarding the form.

The form does not provide any further information or explanation of the sections and elections mentioned.
