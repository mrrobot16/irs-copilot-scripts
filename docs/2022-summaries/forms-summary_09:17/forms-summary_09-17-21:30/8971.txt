This document provides updated information regarding the address to file Form 8971, which is used to report information about beneficiaries acquiring property from a deceased individual. The updated address is the Department of the Treasury, Internal Revenue Service, Stop 8307F, 7940 Kentucky Drive, Florence, KY 41042-2915. The document also includes a form with sections to provide information about the decedent and executor, as well as the beneficiaries and the property they acquired. It emphasizes that only Schedule A of Form 8971 should be provided to the beneficiaries, while the form itself should be submitted to the IRS along with a copy of each completed Schedule A. The document also includes a continuation sheet if additional space is needed to report property acquired by the beneficiaries.
