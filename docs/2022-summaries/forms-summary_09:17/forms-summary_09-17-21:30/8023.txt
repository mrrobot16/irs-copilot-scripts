Form 8023 is a document provided by the Internal Revenue Service (IRS) for corporations making qualified stock purchases and wishing to make elections under Section 338 of the tax code. The form collects information about the purchasing corporation, the common parent of the purchasing corporation, the target corporation, and the common parent of the selling consolidated group, selling affiliate, S corporation shareholder, or U.S. shareholder. It also includes sections for general information and elections under Section 338. The form requires signatures from authorized individuals representing the purchasing corporation, the common parent or selling affiliate, and the S corporation shareholder if applicable.
