This document provides information about Form 1099-S, which is used to report proceeds from real estate transactions to the Internal Revenue Service (IRS). It includes instructions for filers and transferors on how to complete the form and where to find additional resources. It also emphasizes the importance of using the official scannable version of the form and warns against filing forms that cannot be scanned, as penalties may be imposed. The document also provides information about ordering official IRS information returns and filing electronically using the IRS Filing Information Returns Electronically (FIRE) system or the IRS Affordable Care Act Information Returns (AIR) program.
