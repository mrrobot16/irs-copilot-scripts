This document is a form for taxpayers to fill out when applying for a determination for adopters of modified volume submitter plans. The form includes sections for the taxpayer to provide information about their plan sponsor, contact person, determination requested, amendments, plan details, and procedural requirements. The form also includes a checklist for taxpayers to ensure they have included all necessary documentation with their application. The form is for use by taxpayers in the United States and is provided by the Internal Revenue Service (IRS).
