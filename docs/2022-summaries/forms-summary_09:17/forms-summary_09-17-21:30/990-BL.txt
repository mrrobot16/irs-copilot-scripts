Form 990-BL is an information and initial excise tax return for Black Lung Benefit Trusts and certain related persons under section 501(c)(21) of the Internal Revenue Code. The form is used to report revenue, expenses, and balance sheets for the trust. It includes sections for analysis of revenue and expenses, balance sheets, and a questionnaire. The form also includes a schedule for reporting initial taxes on self-dealing and taxable expenditures. The form must be filed annually and is used by the IRS to ensure compliance with tax laws.
