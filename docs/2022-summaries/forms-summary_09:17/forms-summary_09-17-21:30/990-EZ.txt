The document is a Form 990-EZ, which is a short form return for organizations exempt from income tax under section 501(c), 527, or 4947(a)(1) of the Internal Revenue Code (except private foundations). The form is used to report revenue, expenses, changes in net assets or fund balances, balance sheets, program service accomplishments, and other information relevant to the organization's tax-exempt status. The form includes various sections and tables that require the organization to provide details about its financial activities, including contributions, program service revenue, membership dues, investment income, expenses, assets, liabilities, program service accomplishments, and compensation of employees and independent contractors. The form also includes questions about the organization's activities, such as lobbying, political campaign activities, transfers to related organizations, and completion of Schedule A. The form must be signed by an authorized officer and may be prepared by a paid preparer.
