The document is a Schedule K-1 form for the tax year 2022. It is used to report a shareholder's share of income, deductions, credits, and other items from a corporation. The form requires information about the corporation, including its employer identification number, name, address, and the IRS center where it filed its return. It also requires information about the shareholder, including their identifying number, name, address, and allocation percentage. The form includes sections to report various types of income, deductions, credits, and other items, such as ordinary business income, net rental real estate income, interest income, dividends, capital gains, and deductions. There is also a section for alternative minimum tax items and items affecting shareholder basis. The form includes checkboxes for attaching a Schedule K-3 and providing additional information about multiple activities for at-risk and passive activity purposes.
