The document is Schedule K-3 (Form 1120-S) for the tax year 2022. It is used to report a shareholder's share of income, deductions, credits, and other information from a corporation. The form is divided into several parts, including information about the corporation and the shareholder, as well as sections for reporting international information, foreign tax credit limitation, distributions from foreign corporations, information for preparation of Form 1116, information for preparation of Form 8621, and the shareholder's share of S corporation's interest in foreign corporation income. The form includes various checkboxes and tables for reporting specific details and amounts. It is important for shareholders to complete the applicable parts and attach any required additional information.
