Form 1094-B is a document used for the transmittal of Health Coverage Information Returns to the Internal Revenue Service (IRS). It is issued by the Department of the Treasury. The form requires the filer's name, employer identification number (EIN), contact person's name and telephone number, as well as the street address, city, state, country, and ZIP code. The form also includes a section to report the total number of Forms 1095-B that are being submitted with the transmittal. The form must be signed and dated, and the filer declares under penalties of perjury that the information provided is true, correct, and complete. The form includes a notice regarding the Privacy Act and Paperwork Reduction Act, and instructions for completing the form can be found on the IRS website.
