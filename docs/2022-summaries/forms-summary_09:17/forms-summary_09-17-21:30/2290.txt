This document is a coversheet for Form 2290, which is the Heavy Highway Vehicle Use Tax Return. The coversheet provides information about the revision of the form for the tax period from July 1, 2022, to June 30, 2023. It advises taxpayers not to use this revision for tax periods before June 30, 2022, and provides a website where prior revisions can be obtained. The coversheet also includes instructions for filling out the form, including sections for personal information, tax computation, and a statement in support of suspension. The document also includes a Schedule 1 form for reporting heavy highway vehicles and a payment voucher (Form 2290-V) for making a payment by check or money order. It provides instructions for completing the payment voucher and where to send it along with the form and payment.
