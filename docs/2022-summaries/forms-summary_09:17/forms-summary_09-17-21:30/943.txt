Form 943 is the Employers Annual Federal Tax Return for Agricultural Employees. It is used by employers to report their federal tax liability for agricultural employees. The form includes sections for reporting the number of agricultural employees, wages subject to social security and Medicare tax, federal income tax withheld, and various credits and adjustments. The form must be completed and signed by the employer, and any payment due should be made using Form 943-V, the Payment Voucher. The completed form and payment should be sent to the address provided in the instructions.
