Form 709 is the United States Gift (and Generation-Skipping Transfer) Tax Return. It is used to report gifts made during the calendar year and calculate any gift tax owed. The form includes sections for general information about the donor, such as their name, address, and social security number. It also includes sections for reporting gifts made by the donor's spouse, calculating the taxable gifts, and determining the applicable credit against tax. The form also includes schedules for computing taxable gifts, reconciling prior gift tax returns, and calculating generation-skipping transfer tax. The form must be signed by the donor under penalties of perjury.
