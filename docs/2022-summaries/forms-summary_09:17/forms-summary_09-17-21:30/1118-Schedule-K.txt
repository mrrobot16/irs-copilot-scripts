Schedule K (Form 1118) is a form issued by the Internal Revenue Service (IRS) for corporations to reconcile foreign tax carryovers. The form is used for a specific tax year and requires a separate form for each category of income. The form includes various sections to report foreign tax carryovers from previous tax years, adjustments to those carryovers, and the amount of foreign tax carryover used or expired in the current tax year. The form also provides space to report the actual or estimated amount of foreign tax carryover to be carried back to a prior tax year and the total foreign tax carryover to be carried forward to the following tax year. The form includes instructions and a link to the IRS website for additional information.
