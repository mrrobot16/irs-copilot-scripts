Form 8038 is an information return for tax-exempt private activity bond issues. It is used by issuers to report details about the bond issue to the Internal Revenue Service (IRS). The form includes several parts for reporting information such as the issuer's name and employer identification number, the type of bond issue, the use of proceeds, the description of the bonds, and volume caps. The form also includes sections for reporting information about refunded bonds, hedges, reimbursement, and volume limitations. The form must be signed by the issuer's authorized representative and is subject to penalties of perjury. The form is accompanied by separate instructions, which can be found on the IRS website.
