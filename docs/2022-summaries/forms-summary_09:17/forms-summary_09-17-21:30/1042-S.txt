Form 1042-S is a tax form issued by the Internal Revenue Service (IRS) for reporting income subject to withholding for foreign persons with U.S. source income. The form is used to report various types of income, such as interest, dividends, royalties, pensions, and compensation. It includes information about the withholding agent, the recipient of the income, and any other agents involved in the withholding process. The form also includes codes to indicate the type of income, exemption status, and chapter indicator for tax purposes. The form is available in multiple copies for different parties involved in the reporting process, including the IRS, the recipient, and the withholding agent.
