Form W-8EXP is a certificate issued by foreign governments, international organizations, foreign central banks of issue, foreign tax-exempt organizations, foreign private foundations, and governments of U.S. possessions for the purpose of United States tax withholding and reporting. This form is not to be sent to the IRS but rather given to the withholding agent or payer. The form includes sections for the identification of the beneficial owner, qualification statements for Chapter 3 and Chapter 4 status, and a certification section. The form requires the completion of various fields such as the name of the organization, country of incorporation or organization, type of entity, permanent and mailing addresses, U.S. TIN (if required), GIIN, foreign TIN, and reference number(s). The form also includes checkboxes for different Chapter 4 statuses depending on the type of organization. The form must be signed by an authorized official and includes a certification under penalties of perjury.
