Form 4506-B is a document issued by the Internal Revenue Service (IRS) for requesting a copy of an exempt organization's IRS application or letter. The form must be completed clearly and accurately to avoid rejection. The form provides information about where to find exempt organization determination letters issued from 2014 onwards and political organization notices and reports. It also mentions that exempt or political organizations are required to make their returns, reports, notices, and exempt applications available for public inspection. The form includes sections for the requester's information, the exempt organization's information, the category of the requester, the reason for the request, and checkboxes for the specific items being requested. The form also includes a Privacy Act and Paperwork Reduction Act Notice. The form can be submitted electronically via email, although it is important to note that email submissions are not encrypted for security.
