This document is a Schedule C (Form 1065) for partnerships that are required to file Schedule M-3. It provides additional information and questions that need to be answered by the partnership. The questions include whether there were any transfers between the partnership and its partners subject to disclosure requirements, whether there were any disproportionate allocations of income or deductions from another partnership, whether the partnership sold or acquired any interest in an intangible asset from a related person, whether there were any changes in accounting principles for financial accounting purposes, and whether there were any changes in accounting methods for U.S. income tax purposes.
