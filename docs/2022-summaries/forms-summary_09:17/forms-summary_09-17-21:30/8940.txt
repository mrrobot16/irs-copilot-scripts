This document provides important information regarding a change in the mailing address for certain forms related to tax determination. The new mailing address is provided for forms such as 1023, 1024, 1024-A, 1028, 5300, 5307, 5310, 5310-A, 5316, 8717, 8718, and 8940. The document also states that deliveries by private delivery service should be made to a different address. It is emphasized that filers should rely on this update for the change in mailing address, which will be incorporated into the next revision of the forms' instructions. The document then presents Form 8940, which is a Request for Miscellaneous Determination. It includes sections for the identification of the organization, type of request, and required signatures. The document also provides contact information for additional help or inquiries.
