Form 965-D is a document used for transferring tax liabilities under Section 965(i)(2) of the Internal Revenue Code. It is filed with the Department of the Treasury and the Internal Revenue Service. The form includes sections for providing information about the transferor and transferee, as well as the S Corporation involved in the transfer. It also requires details about the triggering event that led to the transfer agreement and the portion of the transferor's deferred net tax liability that is being allocated to the transferred stock. The form also includes a report of the unpaid section 965(i) net tax liability being assumed by the transferee and information about the transferee's ability to pay the remaining liability. The form concludes with a section for the terms of the agreement, including the transferee's agreement to assume the liability and comply with all applicable requirements. The form must be signed by both the transferor and transferee under penalties of perjury.
