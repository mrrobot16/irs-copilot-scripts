Form 1120-FSC is the U.S. Income Tax Return of a Foreign Sales Corporation. This form is used by foreign sales corporations to report their income and taxes to the Internal Revenue Service (IRS). The form includes information about the FSC's name, address, and incorporation details. It also asks for information about the FSC's principal shareholder, parent-subsidiary controlled group status, administrative pricing rules used to determine taxable income, and tax and payment details. The form also includes schedules for reporting cost of goods sold, net income attributable to nonexempt foreign trade income, taxable income or loss, deductions allocated or apportioned to foreign trade income, and liabilities and shareholders' equity. The form also includes schedules for reconciling income per books with income per return and analyzing unappropriated retained earnings per books.
