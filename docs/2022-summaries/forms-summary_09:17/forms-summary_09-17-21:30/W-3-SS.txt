This document provides instructions for filing Forms W-2 and W-3 electronically on the Social Security Administration (SSA) website. It states that fill-in versions of the forms can be created and printed for filing with the SSA, as well as for distribution to employees and filing with state or local governments. It also emphasizes that Copy A of the forms should not be printed and filed from the website, as it is not scannable, and penalties may be imposed for filing forms that can't be scanned. The document includes a form for the transmittal of wage and tax statements (Form W-3SS) and provides instructions for completing and filing it. It also provides information on e-filing options and deadlines, as well as instructions for filing paper forms and the addresses where they should be sent.
