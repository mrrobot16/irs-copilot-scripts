The document is a Form 5498-ESA for the tax year 2021. The form is used to report contributions and rollover contributions made to a Coverdell Education Savings Account (ESA). It consists of three copies: Copy A is filed with the IRS, Copy B is furnished to the beneficiary, and Copy C is for the trustee or issuer. The form includes information such as the trustee or issuer's name and address, the beneficiary's name and address, the account number, and the amounts of contributions and rollover contributions made. The form also provides instructions for the beneficiary and the trustee or issuer on how to complete the form and important deadlines for filing and furnishing the form.
