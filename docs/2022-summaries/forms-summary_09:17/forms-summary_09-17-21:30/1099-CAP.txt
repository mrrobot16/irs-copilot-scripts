This document is the Form 1099-CAP, which is used to report changes in corporate control and capital structure to the Internal Revenue Service (IRS). The form includes information about the corporation, shareholders, and the details of the sale or exchange of stock. It is important for shareholders to report any gain from the exchange on Form 8949, but they cannot claim a loss based on the information provided in Box 2 of the form. The document provides instructions for shareholders and corporations on how to complete and file the form, as well as contact information for assistance.
