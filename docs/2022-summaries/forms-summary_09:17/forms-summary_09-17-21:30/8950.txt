Form 8950 is an application form used for the Voluntary Correction Program (VCP) under the Employee Plans Compliance Resolution System (EPCRS) of the Internal Revenue Service (IRS). The form is filed electronically only on Pay.gov. The form requires the plan sponsor's information, including name, address, employer identification number, and contact details. It also asks for information about the type of VCP submission, plan details such as plan name, plan number, plan year end, and plan assets. The form includes questions about qualification failures, correction methods, retroactive plan amendments, abusive tax avoidance transactions, diversion or misuse of plan assets, and examination status. It also includes a Procedural Requirements Checklist to ensure a complete submission. The form must be signed and dated by an authorized individual.
