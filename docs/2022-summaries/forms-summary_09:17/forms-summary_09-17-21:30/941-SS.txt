Form 941-SS is the Employers Quarterly Federal Tax Return for American Samoa, Guam, the Commonwealth of the Northern Mariana Islands, and the U.S. Virgin Islands. It is used by employers in these territories to report their employment taxes to the IRS. The form is divided into several parts and asks for information such as the number of employees, wages, tips, and other compensation subject to social security and Medicare tax, adjustments, credits, deposits, and refundable credits. The form must be completed and signed by the employer, and any payment should be made using Form 941-V(SS), the Payment Voucher. The form also includes a Privacy Act and Paperwork Reduction Act Notice, which explains the requirements and confidentiality of the information provided on the form.
