This document provides information regarding Form 1098, which is used to report mortgage interest received by recipients/lenders. It states that Copy A of the form is for informational purposes only and should not be printed and filed as it is not scannable by the IRS. Copy B and other copies of the form can be downloaded and printed for providing information to the recipient. Official IRS information returns can be ordered from the IRS website. The document also provides instructions for completing Form 1098 and includes cautionary notes regarding deductibility of mortgage interest, points, and subsidy payments. It also provides contact information for assistance with reporting on Form 1098.
