Form 8288 is a U.S. withholding tax return for certain dispositions by foreign persons. The form is used by the buyer or other party responsible for withholding to report the amount subject to withholding and the withholding tax liability. The form also includes sections for entities subject to the provisions of Section 1445(e) and Section 1446(f)(1) to report their withholding information. Additionally, there is a section for partnerships to report partnership distributions and the total amounts withheld. Finally, there is a section for buyers or transferees claiming a refund of withholding under Section 1446(f)(4) to report the amount subject to withholding, the amount withheld, and the withholding tax liability. The form must be signed by the withholding agent, partner, fiduciary, or corporate officer under penalties of perjury.
