Form 990-T is the Exempt Organization Business Income Tax Return for tax-exempt organizations in the United States. This form is used to report unrelated business taxable income (UBTI) and calculate the tax owed on that income. The form includes sections for providing information about the organization, such as its name, address, and employer identification number. It also includes sections for reporting the organization's total UBTI, deductions, and tax computation. Additionally, the form includes sections for reporting tax credits, payments, and any supplemental information that may be required. The form must be signed by an authorized officer of the organization and is subject to penalties for false statements.
