Form W-7A is an application form used by adoptive parents in the United States to obtain a Taxpayer Identification Number (TIN) for their pending adoption. The form requires the adoptive parent(s) to provide their personal information, including their names, social security numbers, and address. The form also requires information about the child, such as their name, birth information, and placement agency information. The form must be signed by the parent(s) and includes a declaration of accuracy. The form also states that required documentation must be attached to the form. The form includes a privacy notice and instructions on where to find additional information.
