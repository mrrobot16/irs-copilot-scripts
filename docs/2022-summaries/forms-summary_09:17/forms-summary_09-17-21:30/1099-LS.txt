This document provides information and instructions for Form 1099-LS, which is used to report a reportable life insurance sale to the Internal Revenue Service (IRS). It includes copies A, B, C, and D of the form, each with specific instructions for different parties involved in the transaction.

Copy A is provided for informational purposes only and is not scannable if printed from the website. It advises against printing and filing this copy downloaded from the website to avoid penalties. Copies B, C, and D can be downloaded, printed, and used to provide information to the payment recipient and issuer.

The document also provides instructions for payment recipients, issuers, and acquirers on how to complete the form and what information to include. It includes information on taxpayer identification numbers, policy numbers, amounts paid, dates of sale, and contact information.

The document advises acquirers to refer to the current General Instructions for Certain Information Returns and the Instructions for Form 1099-LS for complete guidance on completing the form. It also provides contact information for the IRS information reporting customer service site for any questions or assistance needed.
