Form 1042-S is a document used by the Internal Revenue Service (IRS) to report foreign persons' U.S. source income subject to withholding. The form includes various sections and boxes to report income codes, gross income, tax rates, withholding allowances, net income, federal tax withheld, tax withheld by other agents, and more. It also includes information about withholding agents, recipients, intermediaries, payers, and state income tax withheld. The form provides codes and explanations for different types of income and exemptions. Recipients can attach copies of the form to their federal and state tax returns.
