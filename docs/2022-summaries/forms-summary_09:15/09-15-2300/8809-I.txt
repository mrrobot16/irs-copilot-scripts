Form 8809-I is an application for an extension of time to file the FATCA Form 8966. The form asks for information such as the name of the filer, address, global intermediary identification number (GIIN), taxpayer identification number (TIN), and contact information. It also includes a section to indicate the method of filing the Form 8966 (electronic or paper) and a checkbox for requesting an additional extension if necessary. If requesting an additional extension, the filer must provide a detailed explanation for the need and attach additional pages if necessary. The form must be signed and dated, and the filer declares under penalties of perjury that the information provided is true, correct, and complete.
