Form 1099-QA is a document used for reporting distributions from ABLE (Achieving a Better Life Experience) accounts to the Internal Revenue Service (IRS). The form includes information about the payer, recipient, and the distribution amounts. It also includes checkboxes to indicate if there was a program-to-program transfer or if the ABLE account was terminated in the calendar year. The form provides instructions for recipients on how to report the distribution on their tax return and includes information for payers on how to complete the form. The document also provides contact information for assistance with reporting on Form 1099-QA.
