The document is a set of forms and instructions for filing the Heavy Highway Vehicle Use Tax Return (Form 2290) for the tax period beginning on July 1, 2022, and ending on June 30, 2023. It provides information on how to complete the form, calculate the tax, and submit payment. The document also includes a payment voucher (Form 2290-V) that can be used when making a payment by check or money order. It provides instructions on how to fill out the voucher and where to send it along with the payment.
