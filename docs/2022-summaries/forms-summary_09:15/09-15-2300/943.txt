Form 943 is the Employers Annual Federal Tax Return for Agricultural Employees. It is used to report taxes for agricultural employees. The form includes sections for entering the number of agricultural employees, wages subject to social security tax, social security tax, Medicare tax, additional Medicare tax withholding, federal income tax withheld, and total taxes before adjustments. It also includes sections for adjustments, nonrefundable credits, total taxes after adjustments and nonrefundable credits, total deposits, balance due, and overpayment. The form must be completed in its entirety and signed. There is also a separate payment voucher, Form 943-V, that can be used to make a payment with Form 943. The voucher includes spaces for entering the employer identification number, amount paid, business name, address, and payment details. The voucher should be detached and sent with the payment and Form 943 to the appropriate address.
