This document is Form 1099-LTC, which is used to report long-term care and accelerated death benefits to the Internal Revenue Service (IRS). The form includes various sections for providing information such as the payer's name, address, and telephone number, as well as the policyholder's information and taxpayer identification number (TIN). It also includes sections for reporting gross long-term care benefits paid and accelerated death benefits paid. There are optional sections for indicating if the benefits were from a qualified long-term care insurance contract and if the insured was certified chronically ill or terminally ill. The document includes instructions for policyholders, insured individuals, and payers on how to complete and file the form.
