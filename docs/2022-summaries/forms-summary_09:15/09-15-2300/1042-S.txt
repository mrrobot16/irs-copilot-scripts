Form 1042-S is a document used by the Internal Revenue Service (IRS) to report foreign persons' U.S. source income subject to withholding. It is used to report various types of income, such as interest, dividends, royalties, pensions, and more. The form includes information about the income code, gross income, exemption codes, tax rates, withholding allowances, net income, federal tax withheld, and other details. It also includes information about the withholding agent, recipient, intermediary, and payer. The form provides codes and explanations for different categories and statuses related to withholding and reporting. Recipients may need to attach this form to their federal or state tax returns.
