This document is a Schedule K-1 form for tax year 2022. It is used to report a shareholder's share of income, deductions, credits, and other items from a corporation. The form includes information about the corporation, such as its employer identification number, name, address, and the IRS center where it filed its return. It also includes information about the shareholder, such as their identifying number, name, address, and the number of shares they own. The form has sections for reporting various types of income, deductions, and credits, as well as items that affect the shareholder's basis and other information. There are also checkboxes for indicating if a Schedule K-3 or additional statements are attached. The form includes a notice regarding the Paperwork Reduction Act and provides a website for further information.
