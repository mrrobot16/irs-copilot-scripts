Form 8835 is a document provided by the Department of the Treasury and the Internal Revenue Service for claiming the Renewable Electricity Production Credit on a tax return. The form includes various lines for reporting the electricity produced at qualified facilities using different sources such as wind, closed-loop biomass, geothermal, solar, open-loop biomass, landfill gas, trash, hydropower, and marine and hydrokinetic renewables. The form also includes sections for calculating phaseout adjustments, government grants and credits, additions to the capital account, and determining the final credit amount. The form provides instructions and information on how to complete each line.
