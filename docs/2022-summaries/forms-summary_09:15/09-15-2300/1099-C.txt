This document provides instructions and information regarding Form 1099-C, which is used to report the cancellation of debt. It explains that Copy A of the form is for informational purposes only and cannot be printed and filed with the IRS. Copy B and other copies of the form can be downloaded and printed to provide information to the debtor. The document also provides instructions for debtors on how to report the discharged debt on their tax return and includes information on taxpayer identification numbers, account numbers, and the various boxes on the form. It also provides instructions for creditors on how to complete the form and where to find additional resources.
