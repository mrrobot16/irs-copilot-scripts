This document is a Schedule K-1 form for tax year 2022. It is used to report a partner's share of income, deductions, credits, and other items from a partnership. The form requires information about the partnership, including its employer identification number and contact information. It also requires information about the partner, such as their social security number or taxpayer identification number, name, and address. The form includes sections for reporting the partner's share of profit, loss, and capital, as well as their share of liabilities. It also includes a capital account analysis section and asks if the partner contributed property with a built-in gain or loss. The form further requires the partner's share of net unrecognized Section 704(c) gain or loss, as well as their share of current year income, deductions, credits, and other items. There are additional sections for reporting alternative minimum tax items, tax-exempt income and nondeductible expenses, distributions, and other information. The form also provides space for reporting foreign taxes paid or accrued, as well as more than one activity for at-risk and passive activity purposes. This form is to be filed with the Internal Revenue Service.
