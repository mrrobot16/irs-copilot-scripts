This document is Form 1098-Q, which is used to report information about a Qualifying Longevity Annuity Contract (QLAC) to the Internal Revenue Service (IRS). The form is divided into three copies: Copy A is filed with Form 1096 at the IRS, Copy B is provided to the participant, and Copy C is for the issuer. The form includes sections for providing the issuer's and participant's information, including names, addresses, and tax identification numbers. It also includes fields for reporting the annuity amount on the start date, the annuity start date, whether the start date may be accelerated, total premiums paid, and the fair market value of the QLAC. Additionally, there are boxes for reporting the amount and date of each premium payment made throughout the year. The instructions on the form provide guidance on how to complete it and where to find additional information.
