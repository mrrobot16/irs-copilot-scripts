Form 6069 is a document issued by the Internal Revenue Service (IRS) for the purpose of reporting and paying certain excise taxes on mine operators, black lung trusts, and other persons under sections 4951, 4952, and 4953 of the tax code. The form includes sections for providing information about the filer, such as their name, address, and taxpayer identification number. It also includes sections for reporting and calculating the initial taxes on self-dealing, taxable expenditures, and coal mine operators. The form requires the filer to provide detailed information about the acts of self-dealing, taxable expenditures, and excess contributions. The form must be signed by the trustee, self-dealer, or mine operator and can only be discussed with the preparer if authorized. The latest version of the form can be found on the IRS website along with instructions.
