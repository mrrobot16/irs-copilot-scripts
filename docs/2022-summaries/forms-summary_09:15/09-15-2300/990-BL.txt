Form 990-BL is an information and initial excise tax return for Black Lung Benefit Trusts and certain related persons. It is used to report revenue, expenses, and balance sheets for the trust. The form also includes a questionnaire section that asks about any changes to the governing instrument, taxes on self-dealing and taxable expenditures, and compensation of officers, directors, and trustees. There is also a section for a statement with respect to contributors. The form includes schedules for calculating taxes on self-dealing and taxable expenditures.
