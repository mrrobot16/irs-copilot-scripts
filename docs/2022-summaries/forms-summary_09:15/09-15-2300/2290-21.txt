The document is a collection of forms and instructions related to the filing of the Heavy Highway Vehicle Use Tax Return (Form 2290) for the tax period beginning on July 1, 2021, and ending on June 30, 2022. It provides information on how to fill out the form, calculate the tax, and submit payment. It also includes a payment voucher (Form 2290-V) for those making a payment by check or money order. The document emphasizes the importance of providing accurate information and submitting the forms and payment to the correct address.
