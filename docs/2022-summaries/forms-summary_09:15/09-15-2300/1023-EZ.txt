Form 1023-EZ is a streamlined application for recognition of exemption under Section 501(c)(3) of the Internal Revenue Code. It is used by organizations seeking tax-exempt status. The form requires the organization to provide information about its identification, organizational structure, specific activities, and foundation classification. The form also includes a section for reinstatement after automatic revocation. The form must be completed electronically on Pay.gov and the organization must meet certain eligibility requirements to file this form.
