Form 1066 is the U.S. Real Estate Mortgage Investment Conduit (REMIC) Income Tax Return for the year 2022. The form is used to report taxable income or net loss for REMICs. The document includes sections for computation of taxable income or net loss, tax and payments, additional information, balance sheets per books, and reconciliation of residual interest holders' capital accounts. The form also provides instructions and space to enter relevant details such as the REMIC's assets, liabilities, and capital accounts, as well as information about the partnership representative.
