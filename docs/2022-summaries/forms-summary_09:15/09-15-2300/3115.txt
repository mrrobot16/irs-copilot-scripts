Form 3115 is an application for a change in accounting method for tax purposes. The form requires the filer to provide information such as their name, address, identification number, and principal business activity code. The form also asks for details about the requested change in accounting method, including the type of applicant, the type of accounting method change being requested, and any relevant eligibility rules or restrictions. The form includes various sections for different types of changes, such as depreciation or amortization, financial products, and long-term contracts. The applicant must provide a detailed explanation and supporting documentation for the requested change. The form also includes sections for a section 481(a) adjustment and information about any pending requests or examinations.
