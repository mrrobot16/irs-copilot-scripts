Form 8975 is a document provided by the Department of the Treasury and the Internal Revenue Service (IRS) for reporting purposes. It is used for the Country-by-Country Report and is applicable for the reporting period beginning and ending on specified dates. The form includes sections for the identification of the reporting entity, such as name, reporting role code, and EIN. It also allows for the inclusion of additional information related to the U.S. Multinational Enterprise (MNE) group. The form provides instructions and the latest information on the IRS website.
