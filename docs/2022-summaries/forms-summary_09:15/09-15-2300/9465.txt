Form 9465 is an Installment Agreement Request form provided by the Internal Revenue Service (IRS) for taxpayers who owe taxes and want to set up a payment plan. The form requires personal information such as name, social security number, address, and contact information. It also asks for details about the amount owed and any additional balances due. The form calculates the monthly payment amount based on the total amount owed and provides options for payment methods, such as direct debit or payroll deduction. The form also includes a section for additional information, including questions about marital status, dependents, income, expenses, and other financial obligations. The form must be signed and submitted to the IRS for approval.
