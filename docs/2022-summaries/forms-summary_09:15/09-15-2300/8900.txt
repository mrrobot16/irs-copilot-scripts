Form 8900 is a document provided by the Internal Revenue Service (IRS) for taxpayers to claim the Qualified Railroad Track Maintenance Credit. The form should be attached to the taxpayer's tax return. The form requires the taxpayer to provide information about qualified railroad track maintenance expenditures paid or incurred, as well as the number of miles of railroad track owned or leased by the taxpayer. The form also includes instructions for completing the various sections. The final result is the calculation of the qualified railroad track maintenance credit, which may need to be reported on Schedule K for partnerships and S corporations, or on Form 3800, Part III, line 4g for all others. The form provides a website for instructions and the latest information. The form includes an OMB number and a sequence number for identification purposes.
