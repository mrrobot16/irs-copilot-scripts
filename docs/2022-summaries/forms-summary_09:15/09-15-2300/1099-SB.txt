The document is Form 1099-SB, which is used for reporting the seller's investment in a life insurance contract. It includes information such as the seller's name, address, policy number, investment in the contract, surrender amount, and contact information for the issuer. There are separate copies of the form for the IRS, the seller, and the issuer. The document also provides instructions for both the seller and the issuer on how to complete and file the form.
