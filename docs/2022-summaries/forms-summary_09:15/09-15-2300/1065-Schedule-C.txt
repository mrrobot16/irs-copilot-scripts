This document is Schedule C (Form 1065) for partnerships that are Schedule M-3 filers. It asks a series of questions related to transfers between the partnership and its partners, allocations from another partnership, transfers of intangible assets to related persons, acquisitions of intangible assets from related persons, changes in accounting principles for financial accounting purposes, and changes in accounting methods for U.S. income tax purposes. The document provides checkboxes for "Yes" or "No" answers to each question.
