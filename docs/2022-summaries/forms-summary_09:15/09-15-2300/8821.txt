Form 8821 is a Tax Information Authorization form issued by the Internal Revenue Service (IRS). It is used to authorize individuals or entities to access and receive confidential tax information on behalf of the taxpayer. The form contains several sections that need to be completed, including taxpayer information, designee information, tax information details, and revocation of prior authorizations. The taxpayer must sign and date the form for it to be valid. The form should not be used to request copies of tax returns or authorize representation.
