Schedule F (Form 965) is a document that is used by domestic corporations to report foreign taxes deemed paid. It is specifically for foreign corporations with a section 965(a) inclusion during the 2020 tax year. The form requires the filer to provide information such as the name and identifying number of the person filing the return, the name and identifying number of the Deferred Foreign Income Corporation (DFIC), the U.S. tax year end of the DFIC, and the country of incorporation. The form also includes sections for reporting the section 965(a) inclusion in U.S. dollars and functional currency, post-1986 undistributed earnings in functional currency, opening balance in post-1986 foreign income taxes, foreign taxes paid for the tax year indicated, foreign taxes deemed paid for the tax year indicated, and post-1986 foreign income taxes. The form provides columns for calculations and instructions on how to complete each section. Additional schedules can be added if needed.
