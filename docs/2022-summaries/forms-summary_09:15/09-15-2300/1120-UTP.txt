The document is a Schedule UTP (Uncertain Tax Position) form for Form 1120, which is used for corporate tax returns. The form consists of three parts. 

Part I is for uncertain tax positions for the current tax year. It includes columns for UTP number, primary IRC sections, primary IRC subsections, revenue rulings or procedures, regulation sections, timing codes, pass-through entity EIN, major tax position, ranking of tax position, form or schedule, line number, and amount.

Part II is for uncertain tax positions for prior tax years. It has the same columns as Part I.

Part III is for concise descriptions of the uncertain tax positions listed in Parts I and II.

The form is to be filed with the tax return and is used to report uncertain tax positions that the corporation has taken or plans to take.
