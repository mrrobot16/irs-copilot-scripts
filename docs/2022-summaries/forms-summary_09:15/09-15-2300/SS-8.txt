Form SS-8 is a document used by the Internal Revenue Service (IRS) to determine the worker status for federal employment taxes and income tax withholding purposes. The form requires information about the firm or person for whom the worker performed services, as well as information about the worker themselves. The form also includes sections for disclosing information and completing questions about the worker's job, including their training, work assignments, work location, and payment arrangements. Additionally, there is a section specifically for service providers or salespersons. The form must be completed accurately and signed under penalties of perjury.
