Form 8886 is a Reportable Transaction Disclosure Statement that must be attached to a tax return. The form requires the taxpayer to provide information about reportable transactions, including the type of transaction, any tax benefits generated, and any individuals or entities involved in the transaction. The form also asks for information about fees paid to individuals or entities who promoted or provided tax advice related to the transaction. The form includes spaces for the taxpayer to provide details about the transaction and any tax result protection. Additionally, the form asks the taxpayer to identify any individuals or entities involved in the transaction that are tax-exempt, foreign, or related. The form provides checkboxes and spaces for the taxpayer to provide the necessary information.
