Form 8869 is a document used by a parent S Corporation to make a Qualified Subchapter S Subsidiary Election. The form requires information such as the name and address of the parent corporation, its employer identification number (EIN), and the tax year ending. It also asks for the name and address of the subsidiary corporation, its EIN, and the date of incorporation. The form includes questions about whether the subsidiary has previously filed a federal income tax return, whether the election is being made in combination with a reorganization, and whether the subsidiary's last return was filed as part of a consolidated return. The form must be signed by an officer of the parent corporation.
