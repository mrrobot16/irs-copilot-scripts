Form 8844 is a document provided by the Internal Revenue Service (IRS) for claiming the Empowerment Zone Employment Credit. The form should be attached to the taxpayer's tax return. The form includes various lines for entering information related to qualified empowerment zone wages, calculating the credit percentage, and determining the credit amount for partnerships, S corporations, cooperatives, estates, and trusts. The form also provides instructions for adjustments and allocation of the credit amount. The completed form should be reported on Schedule K or Form 3800, depending on the type of entity.
