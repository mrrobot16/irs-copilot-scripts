This document is a Form 1099-CAP, which is used to report changes in corporate control and capital structure. The form is divided into three copies: Copy A is for the Internal Revenue Service (IRS), Copy B is for the shareholder, and Copy C is for the corporation. The form includes fields for the corporation's name, address, and telephone number, as well as the taxpayer identification number (TIN) for both the corporation and the shareholder. It also includes fields for the date of sale or exchange, the aggregate amount received, the number of shares exchanged, and the classes of stock exchanged. The form provides instructions for the shareholder and the corporation, including information on reporting gains or losses from the exchange and the use of account numbers. The document also includes contact information for the IRS information reporting customer service site for assistance with reporting on Form 1099-CAP.
