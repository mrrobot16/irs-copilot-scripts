Form 8979 is a document provided by the Department of the Treasury, Internal Revenue Service. It pertains to the revocation, designation, and resignation of a partnership representative. The form includes various sections where the reasons for filing can be indicated, such as revoking or designating an entity partnership representative, designating an individual partnership representative, appointing a successor designated individual, or resigning as a partnership representative. The form also requires the completion of information related to the entity or individual involved, including their names, taxpayer identification numbers, addresses, and contact details. The form concludes with signature sections for the authorized person or designated individual, depending on the purpose of filing the form.
