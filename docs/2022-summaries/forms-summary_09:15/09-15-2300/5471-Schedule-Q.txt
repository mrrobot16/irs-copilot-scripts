Schedule Q (Form 5471) is a document that is attached to Form 5471 and is used to report CFC (Controlled Foreign Corporation) income by income groups. The form requires the filer to provide information such as the name of the person filing the form, identifying number, name of the foreign corporation, and EIN (if any). The form also requires the filer to complete a separate Schedule Q for each applicable category of income.

The form includes various sections where the filer needs to enter information related to the income categories, such as dividends, interest, rents, royalties, net gain from property transactions, net gain from commodities transactions, net foreign currency gain, income equivalent to interest, and other income. The form also includes sections for foreign base company sales income, foreign base company services income, full inclusion foreign base company income, insurance income, international boycott income, bribes, kickbacks, and other payments, section 901(j) income, recaptured subpart F income, tested income group, and residual income group.

Each section requires the filer to enter the gross income, definitely related expenses, related person interest expense, other interest expense, research and experimental expenses, other expenses, current year tax on reattributed income from disregarded payments, current year tax on all other disregarded payments, other current year taxes, net income, foreign taxes for which credit is allowed in U.S. dollars, average asset value, high tax election, loss allocation, and net income after loss allocation.

The form provides separate pages for each income group and requires the filer to enter amounts in the functional currency of the foreign corporation, unless otherwise noted. The form also includes instructions and a computer-generated Schedule Q.
