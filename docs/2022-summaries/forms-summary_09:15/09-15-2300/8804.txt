Form 8804 is an annual return for partnership withholding tax under Section 1446. The form requires the partnership to provide information about their name, address, and employer identification number (EIN). It also includes sections for the withholding agent's information and details about the partnership's tax liability and payments. The form calculates the gross section 1446 tax liability and allows for reporting of payments made and credits from previous years. The form also includes sections for estimated tax penalty, balance due, overpayment, refund, and credit for the next year. The form must be signed by a partner or limited liability company member and may require the assistance of a paid preparer.
