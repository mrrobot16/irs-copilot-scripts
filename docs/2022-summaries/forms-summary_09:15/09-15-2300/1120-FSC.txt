Form 1120-FSC is the U.S. Income Tax Return of a Foreign Sales Corporation. It is used to report the income and deductions of a foreign sales corporation for tax purposes. The form requires information such as the corporation's name, address, and identification number, as well as details about its assets, shareholders, and parent-subsidiary controlled group. It also includes schedules for reporting cost of goods sold, taxable income, deductions, and balance sheet information. The form provides a reconciliation of income per books with income per the tax return, as well as an analysis of unappropriated retained earnings. The form is to be filed with the Internal Revenue Service.
