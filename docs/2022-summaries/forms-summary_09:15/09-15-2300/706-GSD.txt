Form 706-GS(D) is a tax return form used for reporting generation-skipping transfer tax for distributions made after December 31, 2010. The form requires the filer to provide general information such as the name and social security number of the distributee, as well as the name and address of the person filing the return. Part II of the form is used to report the distributions made, including the trust's employer identification number and the amount transferred. Part III is used for tax computation, including adjusted allowable expenses, taxable amount, maximum federal estate tax rate, and generation-skipping transfer tax. The form also includes sections for payment, overpayment, and a declaration of accuracy.
