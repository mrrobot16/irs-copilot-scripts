Form 1041 is an income tax return form for estates and trusts in the United States. The form is used to report income, deductions, and tax payments for the calendar year 2022 or fiscal year beginning in 2022. The form requires information such as the name of the estate or trust, the fiduciary's name and title, address details, and employer identification number. The form also includes sections to report various types of income, deductions, and tax computations. Additional schedules may be required for specific deductions or credits. The form must be signed by the fiduciary or an officer representing the fiduciary. The form also includes sections for other information, such as tax-exempt income, foreign financial accounts, and distributions from foreign trusts.
