Form 720 is a Quarterly Federal Excise Tax Return provided by the Department of the Treasury and Internal Revenue Service. It is used to report and pay federal excise taxes on various goods and services. The form includes sections for reporting environmental taxes, communications and air transportation taxes, fuel taxes, foreign insurance taxes, manufacturers taxes, ship passenger tax, and other excise taxes. The form also includes schedules for reporting excise tax liability, two-party exchange information reporting, and claims for refunds or credits. The payment voucher, Form 720-V, is provided for taxpayers making payments by check or money order.
