Form W-7A is an application form for obtaining a Taxpayer Identification Number (TIN) for pending U.S. adoptions. The form is provided by the Department of the Treasury and the Internal Revenue Service. The form includes sections for adoptive parent information, adoptive parent address, child's name and birth information, and placement agency information. The form also requires the signature of the parent and the attachment of required documentation. The form includes a declaration under penalties of perjury that the information provided is true, correct, and complete. The form should be kept for records.
