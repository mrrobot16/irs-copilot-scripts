Form 7004 is an application for automatic extension of time to file certain business income tax, information, and other returns. The form requires the filer to provide their identifying information such as name, identifying number, and address. It also asks for the form code for the specific return that the application is for. The form includes a Part II that must be completed by all filers, which includes checkboxes for indicating if the organization is a foreign corporation without an office in the US or if it is a corporation that intends to file a consolidated return. The form also asks for the tax year and provides space to indicate if it is a short tax year. The form requires the filer to provide the tentative total tax, total payments and credits, and calculates the balance due. The form includes a Privacy Act and Paperwork Reduction Act Notice.
