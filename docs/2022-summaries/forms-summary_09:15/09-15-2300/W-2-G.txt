The document is a Form W-2G, which is used to report certain gambling winnings to the Internal Revenue Service (IRS). The form includes information about the payer, the winner, the amount of winnings, the date won, the type of wager, federal income tax withheld, and other details. There are multiple copies of the form for different purposes, such as filing with the IRS, attaching to a federal tax return, attaching to a state or local tax return, and keeping for the winner's records. The form also includes instructions for the winner and the payer on how to complete and file the form.
