Form 1120-H is the U.S. Income Tax Return for Homeowners Associations. It is used by homeowners associations to report their income and expenses for tax purposes. The form includes sections for providing information such as the association's name, address, and employer identification number. It also requires the association to indicate if it is filing a final return, has undergone a name or address change, or is filing an amended return. The form includes sections for reporting the association's total exempt function income, total expenditures made for qualifying purposes, and total expenditures for the tax year. It also requires the association to report its tax-exempt interest received or accrued during the tax year. The form further includes sections for reporting various types of income, deductions, and tax credits. Finally, the form includes a declaration section for the association's officer to sign, as well as a section for the paid preparer to provide their information if applicable.
