Schedule F (Form 990) is a document that organizations need to complete if they engage in activities outside the United States. It provides information on grants and other assistance provided to organizations or entities outside the United States, as well as grants and assistance provided to individuals outside the United States. The form also asks about any foreign forms that the organization may need to file, such as Form 926, Form 3520, Form 3520-A, Form 5471, Form 8621, Form 8865, and Form 5713. Additionally, there is a section for supplemental information where organizations can provide additional details related to the activities and grants outside the United States.
