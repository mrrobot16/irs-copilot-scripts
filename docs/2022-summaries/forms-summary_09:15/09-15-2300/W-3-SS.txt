The document provides instructions for filing Forms W-2 and W-3 electronically on the SSA's Employer W-2 Filing Instructions and Information web page. It states that fill-in versions of the forms can be created for filing with the SSA, as well as printed out for filing with state or local governments, distribution to employees, and record-keeping purposes. It advises against printing and filing Copy A of the forms downloaded from the website with the SSA, as it may result in a penalty. The document also includes information on ordering official IRS information returns and provides contact information for different tax departments in specific regions.
