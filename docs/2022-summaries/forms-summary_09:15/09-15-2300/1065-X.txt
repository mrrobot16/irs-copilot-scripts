Form 1065-X is a document used by partnerships and certain other entities to file an amended return or request an administrative adjustment. The form is used by filers of Forms 1065, 1065-B, and 1066. The document includes various sections and boxes that need to be filled out depending on the specific circumstances of the partnership.

Part I of the form asks for basic information such as the name, employer identification number, and address of the partnership. It also asks for the number of Schedules K-1 or Schedules Q being filed with the return.

Part II of the form is for partnerships filing Form 1065 only and includes items related to income, deductions, credits, and other information.

Part III of the form is for electing large partnerships (ELPs) and real estate mortgage investment conduits (REMICs) and includes items specific to these types of entities.

Part IV of the form is for calculating the imputed underpayment under the centralized partnership audit regime.

Part V is for providing explanations for any changes made on the amended return or administrative adjustment request.

The form includes instructions and additional information can be found on the IRS website.
