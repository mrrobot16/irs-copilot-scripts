Form 1040-X is an amended U.S. Individual Income Tax Return used to make changes to previously filed tax returns for the year 2019 or later. The form includes sections for personal information, income and deductions, tax liability, payments, and refund or amount owed. It also provides space to explain the changes being made and requires the signature of the taxpayer and, if applicable, their spouse. The form should be completed accurately and kept for record-keeping purposes.
