Form 8883 is an Asset Allocation Statement that is to be attached to an income tax return. It is used for reporting information related to a transaction under Section 338 of the Internal Revenue Code. The form is divided into several parts, including identifying information for the filer, other party, and target corporation, as well as general information about the acquisition. There is also a section for reporting the original statement of assets transferred and a supplemental statement for any amendments. The form includes various checkboxes and spaces for entering specific information related to the transaction.
