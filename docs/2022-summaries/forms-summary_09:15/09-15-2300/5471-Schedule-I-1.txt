Schedule I-1 (Form 5471) is a document provided by the Internal Revenue Service (IRS) for reporting information related to Global Intangible Low-Taxed Income (GILTI). It is to be attached to Form 5471. The form requires the filer to provide their name, identifying number, and the name of the foreign corporation. It also includes sections for reporting gross income, exclusions, deductions, tested income (loss), tested foreign income taxes, qualified business asset investment (QBAI), interest expense, and interest income. The form provides specific instructions for each section and includes spaces for entering the necessary information. The latest version of the form and instructions can be found on the IRS website.
