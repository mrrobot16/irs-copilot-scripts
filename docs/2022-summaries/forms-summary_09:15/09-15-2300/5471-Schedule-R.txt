Schedule R (Form 5471) is a document that is attached to Form 5471, which is used for reporting information about certain foreign corporations. The purpose of Schedule R is to report distributions from a foreign corporation. The form includes sections for providing details such as the description of the distribution, date of distribution, amount of distribution in the foreign corporation's functional currency, and amount of earnings and profits (E&P) distribution in the foreign corporation's functional currency. The form also includes space for listing multiple distributions. The form should be filed by the person filing Form 5471 and should include the name and identifying number of the person filing, as well as the name and EIN (if any) of the foreign corporation. The form also includes a reference ID number, which is explained in the instructions. The form is available on the IRS website, along with instructions and the latest information. The form is subject to the Paperwork Reduction Act.
