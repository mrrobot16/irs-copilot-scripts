Form W-8BEN-E is a certificate of status for beneficial owners of entities for tax withholding and reporting purposes in the United States. The form is to be used by entities, while individuals should use Form W-8BEN. The form includes various sections for identifying the beneficial owner, determining the entity's status for tax purposes, and claiming treaty benefits if applicable. The form also includes sections for specific types of entities, such as sponsored FFIs, retirement plans, nonprofit organizations, and more. The form must be signed by an authorized individual and can be provided to withholding agents or payers.
