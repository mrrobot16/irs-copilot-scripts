Form 8023 is a document provided by the Department of the Treasury, Internal Revenue Service, for corporations making qualified stock purchases. The form requires information about the purchasing corporation, the common parent of the purchasing corporation, and the target corporation. It also includes a section for the common parent of the selling consolidated group, selling affiliate, S corporation shareholder, or U.S. shareholder. The form gathers general information such as the acquisition date and the percentage of target corporation stock purchased. It includes sections for making elections under Section 338, including a section 338(h)(10) election and a gain recognition election. The form requires signatures from authorized individuals representing the purchasing corporation, the common parent or selling affiliate, and the S corporation shareholder(s).
