Form 8938 is a statement of specified foreign financial assets that must be attached to a tax return. It is used to report information about foreign deposit and custodial accounts, as well as other foreign assets. The form includes sections for providing detailed information about each account or asset, such as account numbers, maximum values, and whether foreign currency exchange rates were used. There is also a section for reporting tax items attributable to the specified foreign financial assets. Additionally, the form includes a section for reporting excepted specified foreign financial assets that do not need to be included on Form 8938. The form provides instructions for completing each section and includes space for attaching additional statements if necessary.
