The document is an application form for an IRS Individual Taxpayer Identification Number (ITIN). It is intended for individuals who are not U.S. citizens or permanent residents. The form is used to apply for a new ITIN or renew an existing one. The form includes sections for providing personal information, such as name, address, date of birth, and gender. It also asks for information related to citizenship, foreign tax identification numbers, U.S. visa details, and previous ITIN or IRSN numbers. The form must be signed and dated by the applicant or their delegate.
