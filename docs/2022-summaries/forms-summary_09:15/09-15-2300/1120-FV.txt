Schedule V (Form 1120-F) is a document provided by the Department of the Treasury, Internal Revenue Service. It is to be attached to Form 1120-F and contains a list of vessels or aircraft, operators, and owners. The form requests information such as the name of the vessel or type of aircraft, vessel or aircraft ID number, whether the vessel or aircraft was chartered in or chartered out, the name and employer identification number (EIN) or social security number (SSN) of the registered owner and operator, and U.S. source gross transportation income. There are instructions provided for determining whether additional information needs to be attached to Schedule V for chartered vessels or aircraft. The form also includes calculations for total U.S. source gross transportation income, treaty exemptions, and the resulting amount to be entered on Form 1120-F.
