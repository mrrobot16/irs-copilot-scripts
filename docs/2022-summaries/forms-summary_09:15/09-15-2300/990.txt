The Form 990 is a document that organizations exempt from income tax under sections 501(c), 527, or 4947(a)(1) of the Internal Revenue Code must file with the Internal Revenue Service. It is used to report financial information, activities, governance, and other relevant details about the organization. The form consists of several sections, including a summary, statement of program service accomplishments, checklist of required schedules, governance and management information, statement of revenue, statement of functional expenses, balance sheet, reconciliation of net assets, and financial statements and reporting. It also includes various schedules and instructions for completing the form.
