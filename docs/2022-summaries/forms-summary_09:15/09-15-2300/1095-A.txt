This document provides information about Form 1095-A, which is used by Health Insurance Marketplaces to report enrollments in qualified health plans. The form is completed by the Marketplaces and cannot be completed by individuals. Individuals who receive a completed Form 1095-A from the Marketplace will use the information to file an accurate tax return. The document includes instructions for recipients on how to use the form and complete Form 8962, which is used to claim the Premium Tax Credit. It also provides details on the recipient information, covered individuals, and coverage information sections of the form. The document advises recipients to check the information on the form carefully and contact the Marketplace if there are any questions or discrepancies. It also provides additional resources for information about the Affordable Care Act and the Premium Tax Credit.
