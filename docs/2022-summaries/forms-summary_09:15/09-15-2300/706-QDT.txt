The document is a form titled "Form 706-QDT" issued by the Department of the Treasury and the Internal Revenue Service. It is used for filing the U.S. Estate Tax Return for Qualified Domestic Trusts. The form includes sections for providing general information, making elections, and calculating the tax. It also includes schedules for reporting prior taxable distributions, current taxable distributions, property remaining in trusts at the death of the surviving spouse, marital and charitable deductions. The form is to be completed by the trustee or designated filer of the trust.
