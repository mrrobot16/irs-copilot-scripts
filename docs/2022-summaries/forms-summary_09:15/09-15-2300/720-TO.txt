The document is a form for reporting terminal operator transactions. It includes sections for entering information such as the terminal operator's name, terminal location, transactions for the month, total gallons available, total disbursements, stock gains and losses, and actual physical ending inventory. The form also includes sections for entering receipts and disbursements by product code, destination state, and mode code. The form requires the terminal operator to sign and declare that the information provided is true, correct, and complete.
