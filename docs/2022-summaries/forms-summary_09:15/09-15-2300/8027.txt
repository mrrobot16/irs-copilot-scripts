Form 8027 is the Employers Annual Information Return of Tip Income and Allocated Tips. It is used by employers to report tip income and allocated tips for the calendar year. The form includes sections for the employer's information, establishment details, total charged tips, total charge receipts, service charges paid as wages, tips reported by indirectly and directly tipped employees, gross receipts from food and beverages, and the allocation of tips. Employers must also indicate the method used for the allocation and provide the total number of directly tipped employees at the establishment. The form must be signed and dated by the employer.
