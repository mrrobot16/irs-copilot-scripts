Form 7204 is a document provided by the Department of the Treasury and the Internal Revenue Service (IRS) that allows taxpayers to extend the time to assess tax related to contested foreign income taxes. The form is used to make an election to claim a provisional foreign tax credit for the contested foreign income tax. The form requires the taxpayer to provide their identifying information, including social security number, and the details of the contest, contested foreign income tax, and the payor. The form also includes agreements and declarations that the taxpayer must make regarding compliance with regulations and limitations on assessments and refunds. The form must be signed by the taxpayer and, if applicable, their spouse, representative, or corporate officer.
