Form 1120-IC-DISC is a tax return form for Interest Charge Domestic International Sales Corporations (IC-DISCs). It is used to report the IC-DISC's income, deductions, assets, liabilities, and other relevant information for tax purposes. The form includes various schedules and sections where specific details are provided, such as gross income, cost of goods sold, dividends, inclusions, special deductions, and more. It also includes schedules for reconciling income per books with income per return, analyzing other earnings and profits, accumulated IC-DISC income, and previously taxed income. Additionally, there are sections for reporting export gross receipts, other information, and foreign operations. The form is to be completed and filed by IC-DISCs to comply with tax regulations.
