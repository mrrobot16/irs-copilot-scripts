Schedule R (Form 943) is an allocation schedule for aggregate Form 943 filers. It is used by employers to allocate wages and taxes to each of their clients or customers. The form includes boxes for the employer identification number (EIN), name, type of filer, and report year. It also includes lines for entering amounts allocated to each client, as well as subtotals and totals for various lines on Form 943. The form includes continuation sheets for additional client allocations. The form is to be typed or printed within the boxes, and the instructions should be read before completing the form.
