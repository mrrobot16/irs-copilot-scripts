Form 706-NA is the United States Estate (and Generation-Skipping Transfer) Tax Return for the estate of a nonresident who is not a citizen of the United States. This form is to be filed for decedents who died after December 31, 2011. The form includes sections for providing information about the decedent, executor, and attorney, as well as a tax computation section. It also includes sections for general information and schedules for reporting the gross estate in the United States and calculating deductions. The form must be filed with the Department of the Treasury, Internal Revenue Service Center in Kansas City, MO. The form is to be completed in U.S. dollars and supplemental documents and translations should be attached. The form also includes a declaration of the executor and the option to authorize the IRS to discuss the return with the preparer.
