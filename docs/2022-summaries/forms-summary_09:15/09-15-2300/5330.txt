Form 5330 is a tax return form used to report and pay excise taxes related to employee benefit plans. The form is filed with the Department of the Treasury and the Internal Revenue Service. The form includes various sections for reporting different types of excise taxes, such as taxes on nondeductible contributions to qualified plans, taxes on prohibited transactions, taxes on failure to meet minimum funding standards, and taxes on excess fringe benefits, among others. The form also includes schedules for providing specific details and calculations for each type of tax. The form must be filed by the applicable deadlines specified in the instructions.
