Form 965 is a document issued by the Department of the Treasury and the Internal Revenue Service (IRS) for taxpayers to report the inclusion of deferred foreign income upon transition to the participation exemption system. The form is to be attached to the tax return and can be found on the IRS website along with instructions and the latest information. The form includes various sections for reporting the inclusion of foreign income, deductions, and elections made by the taxpayer. The form also provides space for the taxpayer's identifying information and the tax year to which the form applies.
