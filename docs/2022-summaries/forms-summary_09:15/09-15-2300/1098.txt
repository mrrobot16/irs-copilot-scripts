The document is a form called Form 1098, which is used to report mortgage interest received by a recipient/lender. The form has three copies: Copy A is for informational purposes only and is not scannable when printed from the website; Copy B is for the payer/borrower and contains important tax information to be furnished to the IRS; Copy C is for the recipient/lender. The form includes boxes to report mortgage interest received, outstanding mortgage principal, mortgage origination date, refund of overpaid interest, mortgage insurance premiums, points paid on the purchase of a principal residence, and other relevant information. The form also provides instructions for completing and filing it, as well as contact information for assistance.
