Form 1120-SF is the U.S. Income Tax Return for Settlement Funds under Section 468B. It is used to report income and deductions for settlement funds. The form requires information such as the name and address of the fund, the administrator's information, and details of income and deductions. The form also includes a tax computation section to calculate the tax due or overpayment. Additionally, there is a balance sheet section to report the fund's assets, liabilities, and fund balance. The form also includes additional information sections to provide details about transfers to the fund, tax-exempt interest, distributions to claimants, distributions to transferors or related parties, and the type of liability for which the fund was established. The form must be signed by the fund administrator and may include information about the paid preparer. The form also includes instructions and references to additional forms and resources for further guidance.
