Form 8038-T is a document provided by the Internal Revenue Service (IRS) for reporting arbitrage rebate, yield reduction, and penalty in lieu of arbitrage rebate. It is used by issuers to report information related to these payments and penalties. The form includes sections for reporting the issuer's name, employer identification number, address, and contact information. It also includes sections for reporting the amount and computation date of the rebate and yield reduction payments, penalty in lieu of rebate, late payments, and total payment. The form also includes sections for reporting miscellaneous information such as unspent proceeds, proceeds used to redeem bonds, and fees paid for a qualified guarantee. The form requires a signature from the issuer's authorized representative, declaring that the information provided is true, correct, and complete.
