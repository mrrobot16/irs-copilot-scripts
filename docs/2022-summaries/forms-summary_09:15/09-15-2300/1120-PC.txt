Form 1120-PC is the income tax return form for U.S. Property and Casualty Insurance Companies for the calendar year 2022 or the tax year beginning in 2022 and ending in 2023. The form is used to report taxable income, tax computation and payments, and other information related to the company's financial activities. The form includes various schedules such as Schedule A for taxable income, Schedule B for taxable investment income, Schedule C for dividends and inclusions, Schedule E for premiums earned, Schedule F for losses incurred, Schedule G for other capital losses, Schedule H for special deductions and ending adjusted surplus, Schedule I for other information, Schedule L for balance sheets per books, Schedule M-1 for reconciliation of income per books with income per return, and Schedule M-2 for analysis of unappropriated retained earnings per books. The form also includes sections for providing the company's name, address, employer identification number, and other relevant details.
