Form 1042 is an annual withholding tax return for U.S. source income of foreign persons. The form is used by withholding agents to report the amount of tax withheld and paid on behalf of foreign persons. The form includes sections for recording federal tax liability, total gross amounts reported on Forms 1042-S and 1000, total tax reported as withheld or paid, computation of tax due or overpayment, reconciliation of payments of U.S. source FDAP income, potential Section 871(m) transactions, and dividend equivalent payments by a Qualified Derivatives Dealer (QDD). The form also includes spaces for the withholding agent's information, declaration of preparer, and signature.
