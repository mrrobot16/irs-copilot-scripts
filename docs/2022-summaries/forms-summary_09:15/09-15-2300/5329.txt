Form 5329 is a document issued by the Internal Revenue Service (IRS) that is used to report and calculate additional taxes on qualified retirement plans, individual retirement accounts (IRAs), and other tax-favored accounts. Part VII is used to calculate the additional tax on excess contributions to health savings accounts (HSAs). It requires the taxpayer to report any excess contributions made to their HSAs and calculate the additional tax based on the value of their HSAs.
