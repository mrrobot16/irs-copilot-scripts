This document provides instructions and information for filing Form 2553, which is used to elect S corporation status for a small business corporation under section 1362 of the Internal Revenue Code. The document includes details on where to file the form based on the location of the corporation's principal business, office, or agency. It also provides information on completing the form, including the election information, shareholders' consent statements, and selection of the fiscal tax year. Additionally, the document includes a section for making a Qualified Subchapter S Trust (QSST) election and representations for a late corporate classification election.
