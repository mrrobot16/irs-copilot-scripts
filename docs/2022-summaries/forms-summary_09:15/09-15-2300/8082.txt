Form 8082 is a document used by partners, S corporation shareholders, estate and domestic trust beneficiaries, foreign trust owners and beneficiaries, REMIC residual interest holders, TMPs, and PRs to notify the Internal Revenue Service (IRS) of inconsistent treatment or request administrative adjustments. The form includes sections for general information, identification of the pass-through entity, and details of the inconsistent or adjustment request items. It also provides space for explanations and calculations related to the imputed underpayment and modifications. The form is accompanied by instructions available on the IRS website.
