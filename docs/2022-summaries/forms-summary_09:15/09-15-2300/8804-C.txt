The document is a form titled "Certificate of Partner-Level Items to Reduce Section 1446 Withholding." It is used by foreign partners to certify deductions and losses that can be used to reduce their U.S. federal income tax liability on their share of income or gain from a partnership. The form includes sections for providing partner and partnership information, as well as certifications of deductions and losses. It also includes a section for updating the certificate if there are changes to the information provided. The form must be signed and includes a consent for the IRS to disclose information related to the certificate.
