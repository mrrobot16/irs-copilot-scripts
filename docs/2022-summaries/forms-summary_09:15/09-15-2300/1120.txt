Form 1120 is the U.S. Corporation Income Tax Return for calendar year 2022 or tax year beginning in 2022. The form is used by corporations to report their income and calculate their tax liability. The form includes sections for providing information about the corporation, such as its name, address, and employer identification number. It also includes sections for reporting income, deductions, and credits, as well as balance sheets and reconciliations of income and retained earnings. The form must be filed with the Internal Revenue Service (IRS) and is accompanied by instructions for completing the form.
