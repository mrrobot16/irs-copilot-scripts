Form 1040-SR is a U.S. tax return form specifically designed for seniors. It is used to report income and claim deductions and credits for the tax year 2022. The form includes sections for personal information, filing status, digital assets, standard deduction, dependents, income, adjustments to income, adjusted gross income, deductions, tax and credits, payments, refund, amount owed, and third-party designee. The form also includes a standard deduction chart based on filing status and age/blindness. The form must be signed by the taxpayer and, if applicable, their spouse. The form is accompanied by instructions for completing and filing the form.
