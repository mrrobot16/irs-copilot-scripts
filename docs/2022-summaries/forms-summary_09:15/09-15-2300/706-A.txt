Form 706-A is a document used by the Internal Revenue Service (IRS) to report dispositions or cessations of qualified use under section 2032A of the Internal Revenue Code. It is an additional estate tax return that provides information about the qualified heir, the decedent, and the specially valued property. The form includes sections for general information, tax computation, and schedules for the disposition of specially valued property or cessation of qualified use, as well as involuntary conversions or exchanges, and dispositions to family members of the qualified heir. The form must be completed accurately and signed by the executor.
