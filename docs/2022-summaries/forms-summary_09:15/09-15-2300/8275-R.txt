Form 8275-R is a regulation disclosure statement used to disclose items or positions that are contrary to Treasury regulations. It is to be attached to a tax return and is used when Form 8275 is not applicable. The form requires the disclosure of specific information such as the regulation section, item or group of items, detailed description, form or schedule, and line number. It also includes a section for a detailed explanation and information about a pass-through entity if applicable. The form is used to provide transparency and compliance with tax regulations.
