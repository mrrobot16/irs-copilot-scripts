Form 7203 is a document provided by the Department of the Treasury and the Internal Revenue Service for S Corporation shareholders to report their stock and debt basis limitations. The form includes various sections and lines for shareholders to enter information about their stock basis, debt basis, and allowable loss and deduction items. Shareholders must attach this form to their tax return and can find instructions and the latest information on the IRS website. The form is divided into three parts: Part I for stock basis, Part II for debt basis, and Part III for allowable loss and deduction items. Each part has several lines and columns for shareholders to fill out based on their specific circumstances.
