Form W-12 is an application and renewal form for obtaining a Paid Preparer Tax Identification Number (PTIN) from the Internal Revenue Service (IRS). The form includes various sections for providing personal information, such as name, PTIN, SSN, date of birth, mailing address, and phone number. It also asks for business information, including business address, business identification, and email address. The form requires applicants to disclose any past felony convictions and provide the address of their last U.S. individual income tax return filed. Applicants must indicate their filing status and tax year on their last tax return and confirm their federal tax compliance. The form also includes a section for applicants to declare their awareness of data security responsibilities and disclose any professional credentials they hold. There is a nonrefundable application processing fee of $30.75 for 2023, and the payment must be included with the form. The form can be filed online or by mail, and processing time is typically 4 to 6 weeks.
