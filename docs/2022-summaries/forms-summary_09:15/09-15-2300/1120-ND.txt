Form 1120-ND is a tax return form for nuclear decommissioning funds and certain related persons. The form requires information about the fund, trustee, and disqualified person, as well as details about income, deductions, and tax payments. It also includes schedules for balance sheets and other information. The form is used to calculate and report fund income tax and initial taxes on self-dealing. The form must be filled out accurately and signed under penalties of perjury.
