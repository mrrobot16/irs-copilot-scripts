This document provides information about a form, specifically Form 1028, which is an application for recognition of exemption under Section 521 of the Internal Revenue Code for farmers', fruit growers', or like associations applying for recognition of exemption as cooperatives. The document includes instructions for completing the form and provides important update information, including a new mailing address for certain forms. The form itself includes sections for identification, type of entity and organizational documents, activities and operational information, and financial data. The financial data section includes a statement of receipts and expenditures and balance sheets for the current year and the three immediately preceding years.
