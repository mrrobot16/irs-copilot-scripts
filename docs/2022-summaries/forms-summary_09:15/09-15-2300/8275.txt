Form 8275 is a disclosure statement used by taxpayers to provide additional information about their tax return. It should be attached to the tax return and is not to be used to disclose items or positions that are contrary to Treasury regulations. If the disclosure relates to an information return for a foreign entity, specific information about the foreign entity should be provided. The form has different sections for providing general information, detailed explanation, and information about pass-through entities. It also includes a section for providing additional explanations.
