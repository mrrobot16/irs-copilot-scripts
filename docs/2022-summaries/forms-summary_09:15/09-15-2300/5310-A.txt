This document provides important update information regarding the mailing address for certain forms. The new mailing address is provided for forms such as 1023, 1024, 1024-A, 1028, 5300, 5307, 5310, 5310-A, 5316, 8717, 8718, and 8940. The document also includes a blank form 5310-A, which is a Notice of Plan Merger or Consolidation, Spinoff, or Transfer of Plan Assets or Liabilities; Notice of Qualified Separate Lines of Business. The form includes various sections and lines that need to be completed depending on the purpose of filing the form.
