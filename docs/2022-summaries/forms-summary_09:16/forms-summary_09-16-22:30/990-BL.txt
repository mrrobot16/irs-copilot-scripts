Form 990-BL is an information and initial excise tax return for Black Lung Benefit Trusts and certain related persons under section 501(c)(21) of the Internal Revenue Code. The form is used to report revenue and expenses, as well as balance sheets and questionnaire information. The form also includes schedules for reporting initial taxes on self-dealing and taxable expenditures. The form must be filed annually and is used by the IRS to determine the tax liability of the trust or person filing the return.
