This document provides information regarding Form 1099-C, which is used to report the cancellation of debt. It explains that Copy A of the form is for informational purposes only and cannot be printed and filed with the IRS as it is not scannable. Copy B and other copies of the form can be downloaded, printed, and used to provide information to the recipient. The document also provides instructions for debtors and creditors on how to complete the form and includes information on taxpayer identification numbers, account numbers, and the reporting of discharged debt. It also mentions exceptions and exclusions to including canceled debt in income and provides resources for further information and assistance.
