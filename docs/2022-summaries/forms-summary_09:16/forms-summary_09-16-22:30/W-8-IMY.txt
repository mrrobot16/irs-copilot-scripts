Form W-8IMY is a certificate issued by a foreign intermediary, foreign flow-through entity, or certain U.S. branches for tax withholding and reporting purposes in the United States. The form is used to provide information about the entity, its chapter 3 and chapter 4 status, and its certifications regarding its tax status. The form must be given to the withholding agent or payer and should not be sent to the IRS. The form includes various parts for different types of entities and their specific certifications. The authorized official of the entity must sign the form, certifying that the information provided is true and complete.
