Form 943 is the Employers Annual Federal Tax Return for Agricultural Employees. It is used by employers to report their agricultural employees' wages and taxes to the Internal Revenue Service (IRS). The form consists of three pages and includes sections for the employer's identification number, trade name, address, and number of agricultural employees. It also includes sections for reporting wages subject to social security tax, Medicare tax, and additional Medicare tax, as well as federal income tax withheld. The form includes spaces for adjustments and nonrefundable credits, as well as spaces for reporting deposits and refundable credits. The form must be completed in its entirety and signed by the employer. Additionally, there is a separate payment voucher, Form 943-V, that can be used to make a payment with Form 943.
