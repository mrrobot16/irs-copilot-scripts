Form W-8EXP is a certificate issued by the Department of the Treasury, Internal Revenue Service (IRS) for foreign governments, international organizations, foreign central banks of issue, foreign tax-exempt organizations, foreign private foundations, and governments of U.S. possessions. This form is used for tax withholding and reporting purposes in the United States. The form includes sections for identifying the beneficial owner, qualification statements for Chapter 3 and Chapter 4 status, and a certification section. The form must be provided to the withholding agent or payer and should not be sent to the IRS. The form includes instructions and references to the Internal Revenue Code.
