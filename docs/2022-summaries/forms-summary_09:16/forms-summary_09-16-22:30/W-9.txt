Form W-9 is a document used by individuals and entities to provide their taxpayer identification number (TIN) to the IRS. The form includes sections for the individual or entity's name, business name (if applicable), federal tax classification, exemptions, address, and TIN. The form also includes a certification section where the individual or entity certifies their TIN, tax status, and exemption from backup withholding and FATCA reporting. The form provides instructions for completing each section and includes specific instructions for different types of accounts and entities. The form also includes information on securing tax records from identity theft and provides a privacy act notice.
