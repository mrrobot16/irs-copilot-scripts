This document provides instructions for filing Forms W-2 and W-3 electronically with the Social Security Administration (SSA). It explains that you can create fill-in versions of the forms on the SSA's website and print copies for filing with state or local governments, distributing to employees, and keeping for your records. It also cautions against printing and filing Copy A downloaded from the website with the SSA, as it may not be scannable and could result in a penalty. The document includes a form for completing the necessary information for filing, including the type of payer and employer, number of forms, employer identification number (EIN), wages, taxes withheld, and contact information. It also provides instructions for filing paper forms and where to send them. The document recommends e-filing as a preferred method and provides information on how to do so using the SSA's online services. Finally, it provides specific instructions for filing Copy 1 of Form W-3SS with state, city, or local tax departments in American Samoa, Guam, the U.S. Virgin Islands, and the Commonwealth of the Northern Mariana Islands.
