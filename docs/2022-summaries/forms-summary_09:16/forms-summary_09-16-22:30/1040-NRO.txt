Schedule OI (Form 1040-NR) is a document that taxpayers must attach to their Form 1040-NR when filing their taxes for the year 2022. It contains a series of questions that require the taxpayer to provide additional information related to their citizenship, residency, immigration status, presence in the United States, filing history, trust ownership, compensation, income exempt from tax under a treaty, and elections regarding income from real property located in the United States. The taxpayer must answer all the questions and provide any necessary attachments or documentation as instructed. The purpose of Schedule OI is to gather relevant information that may affect the taxpayer's tax liability and compliance with IRS regulations.
