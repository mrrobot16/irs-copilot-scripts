Form 1099-Q is a tax form used to report payments from qualified education programs under Sections 529 and 530 of the Internal Revenue Code. It is used to report distributions made from qualified tuition programs (QTPs) and Coverdell education savings accounts (CESAs). The form includes information about the payer/trustee, recipient, account number, gross distribution, earnings, basis, trustee-to-trustee transfers, and whether the distribution is from a qualified tuition program or a private or state Coverdell ESA. The form also includes instructions for recipients on how to determine the taxability of the distributions and information for payers/trustees on how to complete and file the form.
