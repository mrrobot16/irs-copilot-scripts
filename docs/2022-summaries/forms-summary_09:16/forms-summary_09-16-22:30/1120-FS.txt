Schedule S (Form 1120-F) is a form used by foreign corporations to exclude income from the international operation of ships or aircraft under Section 883 of the Internal Revenue Code. The form is attached to Form 1120-F and requires the corporation to provide information about its qualified foreign status, the type of equivalent exemption granted by the foreign country of organization, and the gross income in various categories for which the exemption is being claimed. The form also includes sections for the stock ownership test, where the corporation must indicate the test under which the stock ownership test was satisfied and provide information about the stock ownership and qualification of shareholders. The form provides specific instructions for each section and includes checkboxes and spaces for the corporation to fill in the required information.
