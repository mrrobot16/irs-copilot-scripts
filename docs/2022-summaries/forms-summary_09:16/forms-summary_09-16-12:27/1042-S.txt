Form 1042-S is a tax form used by withholding agents to report income paid to foreign persons that is subject to withholding in the United States. The form includes various boxes and codes to report specific information about the income, withholding, and recipient. It is used to report different types of income, such as interest, dividends, royalties, and compensation, as well as the withholding allowance, net income, and federal tax withheld. The form also includes information about the withholding agent, recipient, and any other agents involved in the withholding process. There are different copies of the form for different purposes, such as filing with the IRS, providing to the recipient, attaching to a federal tax return, or attaching to a state tax return. The form also provides explanations of the codes used to report specific information.
