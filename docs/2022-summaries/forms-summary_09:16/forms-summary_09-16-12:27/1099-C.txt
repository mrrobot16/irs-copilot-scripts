This document provides information about Form 1099-C, which is used to report the cancellation of debt. It explains that Copy A of the form is for informational purposes only and cannot be scanned if printed from the website. It warns against filing this copy with the IRS as it may result in penalties. However, Copy B and other copies of the form can be downloaded and printed for providing information to the recipient. The document also provides instructions for debtors and creditors on how to complete the form and includes information about the taxpayer identification number, account number, date of identifiable event, amount of debt discharged, interest, debt description, personal liability for repayment, identifiable event code, and fair market value of property. It also mentions exceptions and exclusions for including canceled debt in income, provides contact information for inquiries, and offers resources for further information and assistance.
