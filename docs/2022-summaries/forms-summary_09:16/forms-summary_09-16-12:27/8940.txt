This document provides important update information regarding the mailing address for certain forms. The new mailing address is provided for forms such as 1023, 1024, 1024-A, 1028, 5300, 5307, 5310, 5310-A, 5316, 8717, 8718, and 8940. The document also mentions that deliveries by private delivery service should be made to a different address. The update is intended to supplement the instructions for these forms and filers should rely on this update for the change described. The document then provides a blank Form 8940, which is a Request for Miscellaneous Determination form used for tax purposes. The form includes sections for identifying the organization, providing contact information, and selecting the type of request being made. The form also includes a declaration section to be signed by an authorized official.
