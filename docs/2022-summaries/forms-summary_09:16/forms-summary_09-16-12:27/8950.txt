Form 8950 is an application form used to apply for the Voluntary Correction Program (VCP) under the Employee Plans Compliance Resolution System (EPCRS) offered by the Internal Revenue Service (IRS). The form must be filed electronically on Pay.gov and is used to correct qualification failures in retirement plans. The form requires information such as the name and address of the plan sponsor, employer identification number, contact information, and details about the plan, including its type, plan number, and assets. The form also includes a checklist of procedural requirements that must be met when submitting the application. The form must be signed and dated by an authorized employee or the owner of the plan sponsor. Additional filing information and instructions can be found on the IRS website.
