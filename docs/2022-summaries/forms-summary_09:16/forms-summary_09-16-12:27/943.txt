Form 943 is the Employers Annual Federal Tax Return for Agricultural Employees. It is used by employers to report their agricultural employees and calculate the taxes owed. The form includes sections for reporting the number of employees, wages subject to social security and Medicare taxes, federal income tax withheld, and total taxes owed. It also includes sections for reporting adjustments, nonrefundable credits, deposits, and refundable credits. The form must be completed in its entirety and signed by the employer. Form 943-V is a payment voucher that can be used to make a payment with Form 943. It includes spaces to enter the employer identification number, payment amount, business name, address, and other relevant information. The voucher should be detached and sent with the payment and Form 943 to the provided address.
