Form 5500-EZ is an annual return form that must be filed by a one-participant retirement plan or a foreign plan. It is required under section 6058(a) of the Internal Revenue Code. The form includes several sections for the identification information of the plan, basic plan information such as the name and effective date of the plan, financial information including total plan assets and liabilities, contributions received, plan characteristics, and compliance and funding questions. The form must be completed accurately and signed by the employer or plan administrator under penalties of perjury.
