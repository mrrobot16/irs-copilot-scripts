Form 990-BL is an information and initial excise tax return for Black Lung Benefit Trusts and certain related persons under section 501(c)(21) of the Internal Revenue Code. The form requires the trust or other person filing the return to provide information about their revenue and expenses, as well as their balance sheets. It also includes a questionnaire section asking about any changes to the governing instrument, self-dealing transactions, and taxable expenditures. The form also includes a section for reporting contributors and a schedule for calculating taxes on self-dealing and taxable expenditures. The form must be signed by an officer or trustee and is subject to penalties of perjury.
