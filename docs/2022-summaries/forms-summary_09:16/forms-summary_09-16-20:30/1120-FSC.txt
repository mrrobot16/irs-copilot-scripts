Form 1120-FSC is the U.S. Income Tax Return of a Foreign Sales Corporation. This form is used by foreign sales corporations to report their income and deductions for tax purposes. The form requires information such as the corporation's name, address, and employer identification number, as well as details about its assets, shareholders, and parent-subsidiary controlled group. The form also includes schedules for reporting cost of goods sold, taxable income or loss, deductions allocated to foreign trade income, and balance sheets. The form also includes schedules for reconciling income per books with income per return and analyzing unappropriated retained earnings per books.
