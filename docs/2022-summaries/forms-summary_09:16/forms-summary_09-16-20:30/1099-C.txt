This document provides instructions and information regarding Form 1099-C, which is used to report the cancellation of debt. The document emphasizes that Copy A of the form should not be printed from the website and filed with the IRS, as it is not scannable. Instead, official IRS information returns should be ordered from the IRS website. Copies B and C of the form can be downloaded and printed for the debtor and creditor, respectively. The document also provides instructions for debtors on how to report the discharged debt on their tax return, and it provides information on the various boxes on the form and their meanings. The document also includes contact information for assistance with reporting on Form 1099-C.
