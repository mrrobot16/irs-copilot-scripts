This document provides instructions for filing Forms W-2 and W-3 electronically with the Social Security Administration (SSA). It states that the forms can be created and filled in online, and printed for filing with state or local governments, distribution to employees, and record-keeping purposes. It also emphasizes that Copy A of the form should not be printed and filed with the SSA as it is not scannable, and penalties may be imposed for filing forms that can't be scanned. The document includes a sample Form W-3SS, which is used as a transmittal for filing paper copies of Forms W-2AS, W-2CM, W-2GU, or W-2VI. It provides information on when and where to file paper forms, as well as options for e-filing. The document also includes instructions for completing the forms and contact information for state, city, or local tax departments.
