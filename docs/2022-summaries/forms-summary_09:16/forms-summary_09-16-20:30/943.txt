Form 943 is the Employers Annual Federal Tax Return for Agricultural Employees. It is used by employers to report wages and taxes for agricultural employees. The form includes sections for entering the employer's name, address, and employer identification number (EIN). It also includes sections for reporting the number of agricultural employees, wages subject to social security tax, social security tax, wages subject to Medicare tax, Medicare tax, additional Medicare tax withholding, federal income tax withheld, total taxes before adjustments, current year adjustments, total taxes after adjustments, and various credits and adjustments. The form also includes a section for reporting total deposits and refundable credits, balance due or overpayment, and a monthly summary of federal tax liability. The form must be completed and signed by the employer. A payment voucher, Form 943-V, is also included for making payments with Form 943.
