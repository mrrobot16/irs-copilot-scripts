Schedule D (Form 965) is a document that U.S. shareholders attach to Form 965 when filing their tax returns. It is used to report the aggregate foreign cash position of specified foreign corporations. The form includes various columns where the cash position at the close of the last taxable year, pro rata share, and other relevant information are entered. The form also provides instructions and a website for further information. Privacy Act and Paperwork Reduction Act notices are included, and additional schedules can be added if necessary. The second page of the form includes calculations to determine the aggregate foreign cash position taken into account on the 2017 and 2018 tax returns.
