Form W-8EXP is a certificate used by foreign governments, international organizations, foreign central banks, foreign tax-exempt organizations, foreign private foundations, and governments of U.S. possessions for United States tax withholding and reporting purposes. The form is not to be sent to the IRS but should be given to the withholding agent or payer. The form includes sections for the identification of the beneficial owner, qualification statements for Chapter 3 and Chapter 4 status, and a certification section. The form requires the signature of an authorized official and includes a declaration under penalties of perjury.
