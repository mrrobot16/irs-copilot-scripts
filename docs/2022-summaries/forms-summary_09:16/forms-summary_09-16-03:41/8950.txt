Form 8950 is an application form used for the Voluntary Correction Program (VCP) under the Employee Plans Compliance Resolution System (EPCRS). It is filed electronically only on Pay.gov. The form requires information such as the name and address of the plan sponsor, contact information, plan details, and information about the qualification failures and correction methods being resolved. The form must be signed and dated by an authorized employee or the owner of the plan sponsor. The document also includes a Procedural Requirements Checklist to ensure that all necessary information and attachments are included in the submission. Additional filing information can be found at www.irs.gov/form8950.
