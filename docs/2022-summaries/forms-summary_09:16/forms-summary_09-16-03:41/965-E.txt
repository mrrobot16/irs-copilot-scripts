Form 965-E is a consent agreement form issued by the Department of the Treasury, Internal Revenue Service. It is used for shareholders who need to make a section 965(h) election. The form requires shareholder information, identifying information of the S Corporation, details of the triggering event, and reporting of unpaid section 965(i) net tax liability. It also includes questions regarding the shareholder's ability to make payments and their leverage ratio. The form concludes with a terms of agreement section, where the shareholder agrees to comply with all the conditions and requirements of section 965(h) and regulations. The form must be signed by the shareholder and includes a declaration under penalties of perjury.
