This document is Schedule G-1 (Form 5471) from the Department of the Treasury, Internal Revenue Service. It is used to report information related to a cost sharing arrangement (CSA) in which a foreign corporation was a participant during the tax year. The form requires the filer to provide a description of the CSA, indicate if the foreign corporation became a participant during the tax year, and state the foreign corporation's share of reasonably anticipated benefits for the CSA. It also asks about platform contributions made by a U.S. taxpayer, stock-based compensation deductions, and intangible development costs. The form must be completed separately for each CSA.
