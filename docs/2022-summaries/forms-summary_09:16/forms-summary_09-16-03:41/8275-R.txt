Form 8275-R is a document used for disclosing items or positions that are contrary to Treasury regulations in relation to tax returns. It is to be attached to the tax return and is used when Form 8275 is not applicable. The form includes sections for providing general information about the regulation section, item or group of items, detailed description, form or schedule, and line number. There is also a section for providing a detailed explanation. If the disclosure is related to a pass-through entity, there is a separate section to be completed by partners, shareholders, beneficiaries, or residual interest holders. The form also includes a section for providing additional explanations.
