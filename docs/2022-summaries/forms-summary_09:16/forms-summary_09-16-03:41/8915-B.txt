Form 8915-B is a document issued by the Department of the Treasury and Internal Revenue Service for reporting qualified 2017 disaster retirement plan distributions and repayments. It is to be attached to the taxpayer's 2021 Form 1040, 1040-SR, or 1040-NR. The form includes sections for reporting distributions from retirement plans other than IRAs and distributions from traditional, SEP, SIMPLE, and Roth IRAs. It also includes sections for calculating excess repayments from 2020, amounts carried back to prior years, and total repayments made in 2021. The form must be signed by the taxpayer and, if applicable, the preparer.
