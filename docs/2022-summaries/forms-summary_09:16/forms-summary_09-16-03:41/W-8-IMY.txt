Form W-8IMY is a certificate used for foreign intermediaries, foreign flow-through entities, or certain U.S. branches to claim withholding and reporting status for U.S. tax purposes. The form is not to be sent to the IRS but should be given to the withholding agent or payer. The form provides various checkboxes for different entity types and their corresponding certifications. The form also includes sections for identification of the entity, chapter 3 and chapter 4 status certifications, and other relevant information. The form must be signed by an authorized official and includes a certification under penalties of perjury.
