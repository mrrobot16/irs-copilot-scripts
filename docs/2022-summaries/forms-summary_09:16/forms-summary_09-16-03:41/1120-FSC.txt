Form 1120-FSC is a U.S. Income Tax Return form for a Foreign Sales Corporation (FSC). The form is used to report the FSC's income and deductions for the tax year. The form includes sections for reporting the FSC's assets, liabilities, and shareholders' equity. It also includes schedules for reporting the FSC's cost of goods sold, taxable income, and deductions allocated or apportioned to foreign trade income. The form also includes schedules for reconciling the FSC's income per books with its income per return, and analyzing its unappropriated retained earnings per books. The form provides instructions for completing each section and schedule.
