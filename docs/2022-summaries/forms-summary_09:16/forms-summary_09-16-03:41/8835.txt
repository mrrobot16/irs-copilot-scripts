Form 8835 is a document issued by the Department of the Treasury and the Internal Revenue Service (IRS) for taxpayers to claim the Renewable Electricity Production Credit. The form must be attached to the taxpayer's tax return. The form includes various lines for the taxpayer to enter information about the electricity produced at qualified facilities using different sources such as wind, closed-loop biomass, geothermal, solar, open-loop biomass, landfill gas, trash, hydropower, and marine and hydrokinetic renewables. The form also includes lines for calculating the credit amount, phaseout adjustment, reduction for government grants and other credits, specified adjustments, special adjustments, and more. The form provides instructions for each line and includes spaces for the taxpayer to enter the necessary information.
