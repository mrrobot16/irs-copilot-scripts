Form 990-BL is an information and initial excise tax return for Black Lung Benefit Trusts and certain related persons under section 501(c)(21) of the Internal Revenue Code. The form is used to report revenue and expenses, as well as balance sheets and liabilities and net assets. It also includes a questionnaire section to report any changes to the governing instrument or other similar instrument, as well as taxes on self-dealing and taxable expenditures. The form also includes a section for a summary of taxes. The form is not open for public inspection.
