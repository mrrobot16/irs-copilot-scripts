Form 941-SS is the Employers Quarterly Federal Tax Return for American Samoa, Guam, the Commonwealth of the Northern Mariana Islands, and the U.S. Virgin Islands. It is used to report employment taxes, including social security and Medicare taxes, for each quarter of the year. The form includes sections for providing employer identification information, reporting the number of employees and their wages, calculating social security and Medicare taxes, and claiming tax credits. The form must be completed and signed by the employer, and any payments should be made using Form 941-V(SS), the Payment Voucher. The form also includes information about the Privacy Act and Paperwork Reduction Act, as well as estimated average times for completing and filing the form.
