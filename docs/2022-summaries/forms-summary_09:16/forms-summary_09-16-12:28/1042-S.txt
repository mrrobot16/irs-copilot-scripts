Form 1042-S is a tax form used by withholding agents to report income paid to foreign persons that is subject to withholding in the United States. The form includes various boxes to report information such as income code, gross income, exemption code, tax rate, withholding allowance, net income, federal tax withheld, tax withheld by other agents, and total withholding credit. It also includes sections to provide information about the withholding agent, recipient, intermediary, and payer, including their names, addresses, tax identification numbers, and chapter status codes. The form is used for federal tax purposes and may need to be attached to federal or state tax returns filed by the recipient or withholding agent. The form includes instructions and codes to help accurately report the income and withholding information.
