Form 1120-FSC is the U.S. Income Tax Return of a Foreign Sales Corporation. This form is used by foreign sales corporations to report their income and calculate their tax liability to the Internal Revenue Service (IRS). The form requires information such as the corporation's name, address, and employer identification number, as well as details about its assets, shareholders, and parent-subsidiary relationships. It also includes schedules for reporting cost of goods sold, taxable income, deductions, and balance sheet information. The form is accompanied by instructions that provide further guidance on completing the form accurately.
