Form 1120-FSC is the U.S. Income Tax Return of a Foreign Sales Corporation. It is used by foreign sales corporations to report their income and calculate their tax liability in the United States. The form includes various sections and schedules to report information such as the corporation's assets, liabilities, income, deductions, and shareholders' equity. It also includes schedules for reconciling income per books with income per return and analyzing unappropriated retained earnings. The form is accompanied by instructions that provide guidance on how to complete it.
