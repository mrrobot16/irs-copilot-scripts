Form 8950 is an application form used for the Voluntary Correction Program (VCP) under the Employee Plans Compliance Resolution System (EPCRS) of the Internal Revenue Service (IRS). The form is filed electronically only on Pay.gov. The form requires the plan sponsor's information, including name, address, employer identification number, and contact details. It also asks for information about the type of VCP submission, plan details such as plan name, plan number, plan year end, assets, and participants. The form includes various checkboxes and questions related to the correction methods, qualification failures, plan examination, previous determination letter applications, and more. The form also includes a Procedural Requirements Checklist to ensure that all necessary documents and information are included with the submission. The form must be signed and dated by an authorized employee or the owner of the plan sponsor. The form is accompanied by instructions and can be found on the IRS website.
