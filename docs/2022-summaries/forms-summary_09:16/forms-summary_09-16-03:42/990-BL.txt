Form 990-BL is an information and initial excise tax return for Black Lung Benefit Trusts and certain related persons under section 501(c)(21) of the Internal Revenue Code. The form requires the trust or person filing the return to provide information about their revenue and expenses, as well as their balance sheets. The form also includes a questionnaire section asking about any changes to the governing instrument, taxes on self-dealing, and taxes on taxable expenditures. The form concludes with a summary of taxes section where the amount of tax due is calculated.
