Form 943 is the Employers Annual Federal Tax Return for Agricultural Employees. It is used by employers to report and pay federal taxes for agricultural employees. The form includes sections for entering the number of agricultural employees, wages subject to social security and Medicare tax, federal income tax withheld, and total taxes before adjustments. It also includes sections for reporting adjustments, nonrefundable credits, deposits, and refundable credits. The form must be completed in its entirety and signed by the taxpayer. A separate payment voucher, Form 943-V, is provided for making payments with the form.
