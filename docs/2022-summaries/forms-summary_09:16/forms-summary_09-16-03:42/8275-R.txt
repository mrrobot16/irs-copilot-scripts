Form 8275-R is a disclosure statement used to report items or positions on a tax return that are contrary to Treasury regulations. This form is specifically for disclosing information related to foreign entities. It must be attached to the tax return and includes sections for providing general information, detailed explanations, and information about pass-through entities. Partners, shareholders, beneficiaries, or residual interest holders must complete Part III if they are making adequate disclosure for a pass-through item. Part IV is for providing additional explanations. The form includes instructions and a reference ID number.
