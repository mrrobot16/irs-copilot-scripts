<h2>Summary of Instructions for Form 965 (Rev. January 2021)</h2>
<p>Form 965 is used to report the inclusion of deferred foreign income upon transition to a participation exemption system as amended by the Tax Cuts and Jobs Act (TCJA). The form and its instructions provide guidance for taxpayers who must include in their income the deferred foreign income of certain foreign corporations.</p>

<h3>General Instructions</h3>
<ul>
  <li><b>Purpose of Form:</b> To report the 2020 tax year share of section 965(a) inclusions from pass-through entities, the 2020 tax year share of section 965(c) deductions from pass-through entities, and the 2020 tax year share of foreign taxes deemed paid and disallowed under section 965(g) with respect to foreign taxes deemed paid in connection with the 2020 tax year share of section 965(a) inclusions from pass-through entities.</li>
  <li><b>Who Must File:</b> Any person with a 2020 tax year inclusion under section 965(a) due to direct or indirect partnership in a domestic partnership, S corporation, or beneficiary of another pass-through entity.</li>
  <li><b>When and Where To File:</b> Attach Form 965 and any required schedules to the income tax return and file by the due date for that return.</li>
  <li><b>Electronic Filing:</b> If filing electronically, include Form 965 and schedules with the electronic return.</li>
  <li><b>Corrections:</b> To correct previously filed Form 965, file a corrected form with an amended tax return and write "corrected" at the top of the form.</li>
</ul>

<h3>Specific Instructions</h3>
<p>The form includes several parts, with specific instructions for each:</p>
<ul>
  <li><b>Part I:</b> Section 965(a) Inclusion</li>
  <li><b>Part II, Section 1:</b> Section 965(c) Deduction by Tax Year</li>
  <li><b>Part III:</b> Elections</li>
  <li><b>Schedule F:</b> Foreign Taxes Deemed Paid by Domestic Corporation (2020 Tax Year)</li>
  <li><b>Schedule H, Section 1:</b> Amounts Reported on Forms 1116 and 1118 and Disallowed Foreign Taxes</li>
</ul>

<p>The form also includes reserved sections for future use and provides details on how to complete each section, including how to report the section 965(a) inclusion and section 965(c) deduction amounts. It specifies the need to attach additional schedules if a taxpayer has inclusions from multiple pass-through entities or if the information exceeds the space provided on the form.</p>

<h3>What's New</h3>
<ul>
  <li>For 2020 tax years, Form 965 is primarily used for section 965(a) inclusions derived through interests in pass-through entities that are U.S. shareholders of deferred foreign income corporations (DFICs).</li>
  <li>Revisions to Schedule F and Schedule H have been made, including the deletion of certain lines and instructions for reporting amounts with respect to section 965(a) inclusions through pass-through entities.</li>
</ul>

<h3>Definitions</h3>
<ul>
  <li>Several key terms are defined, including Controlled Foreign Corporation (CFC), Deferred Foreign Income Corporation (DFIC), Specified Foreign Corporation (SFC), and United States Shareholder (U.S. shareholder).</li>
</ul>

<p>For the latest information about developments related to Form 965, its schedules, and instructions, taxpayers are directed to visit the IRS website.</p>
