<h2>Specific Instructions for Form 1099-R</h2>
<p>Form 1099-R is used for reporting distributions from pensions, annuities, retirement plans, IRAs, or insurance contracts. It includes information on the gross distribution, the taxable amount, the federal income tax withheld, and any capital gain or employee contributions.</p>

<h3>Codes for Box 7</h3>
<p>Box 7 requires distribution codes that indicate the type of distribution received. The instructions provide a detailed list of codes to use, such as early distribution, rollover, disability, death, and others.</p>

<h2>Specific Instructions for Form 5498</h2>
<p>Form 5498 is used for reporting contributions to IRAs, including traditional, SEP, SIMPLE, and Roth IRAs. It includes information on rollover contributions, conversions, recharacterizations, and the fair market value (FMV) of the account.</p>

<h3>Reporting Contributions</h3>
<p>Contributions should be reported for the year they are made. For contributions between January 1 and April 15, trustees and issuers should obtain the participant's designation for the year.</p>

<h3>Required Minimum Distributions (RMDs)</h3>
<p>Trustees must provide a statement to the IRA participant by January 31 regarding the RMD for the year, if applicable.</p>

<h3>Inherited IRAs</h3>
<p>In the year an IRA participant dies, separate Forms 5498 and statements must be provided for the decedent and each nonspouse beneficiary.</p>

<h3>Disaster Relief Reporting</h3>
<p>Special reporting instructions may apply for federally declared disaster areas.</p>

<h3>Combat Zone Participants</h3>
<p>Participants serving in designated combat zones have additional time to make IRA contributions for prior years.</p>

<h3>Repayment of Distributions</h3>
<p>Repayment of qualified reservist distributions, qualified disaster distributions, and qualified birth or adoption distributions should be reported accordingly.</p>

<h2>General Instructions</h2>
<p>Form 1099-R and 5498 filers must adhere to specific IRS guidelines, including deadlines, electronic filing requirements, and furnishing statements to recipients.</p>
