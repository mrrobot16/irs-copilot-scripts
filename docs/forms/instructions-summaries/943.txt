<h3>2022 Instructions for Form 943</h3>
<p>The <b>Internal Revenue Service (IRS)</b> provides instructions for Form 943, the Employer's Annual Federal Tax Return for Agricultural Employees. This form is used by employers in the agricultural sector to report federal income tax withheld and employer and employee shares of social security and Medicare taxes for farmworkers.</p>

<h3>Filing Deadlines</h3>
<p>Form 943 must be filed by <b>January 31, 2023</b>. If deposits are made in full and on time, the deadline extends to <b>February 10, 2023</b>.</p>
