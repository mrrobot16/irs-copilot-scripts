<h2>Summary of Document Regarding Form 7200</h2>
<p>Form 7200, titled <q>Advance Payment of Employer Credits Due to COVID-19</q>, is no longer available for filing as of January 31, 2022. This form was used by employers to request an advance payment of tax credits for COVID-19 related employment benefits. Although the form is not available for current use, it can still be found on the IRS website as a historical reference at the following URL: <a href="https://www.irs.gov/forms-pubs/prior-year" target="_blank">www.IRS.gov/AllForms</a>.</p>
