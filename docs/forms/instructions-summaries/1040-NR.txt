<h1>Summary of 2022 Instructions for Form 1040-NR</h1>
<p>The Form 1040-NR is used by nonresident aliens who engaged in trade or business in the U.S. or earned income from U.S. sources. The form and its instructions are provided by the IRS and can be found on their website.</p>
